<p><b>Crystal Caines</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Guyanese_Americans" title="Guyanese Americans">Guyanese</a>–American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a>, record producer, artist, songwriter and <a href="/wiki/Audio_engineer" title="Audio engineer">engineer</a> based in <a href="/wiki/Harlem,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Harlem, New York">Harlem, New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-nadeska_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nadeska-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> She has Produced and been featured on tracks by <a href="/wiki/Bia_(rapper)" title="Bia (rapper)">BIA</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-nostro_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nostro-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Baauer" title="Baauer">Baauer</a>, <a href="/wiki/M.I.A._(rapper)" title="M.I.A. (rapper)">MIA</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jack_Harlow" title="Jack Harlow">Jack Harlow</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bryson_Tiller" title="Bryson Tiller">Bryson Tiller</a>, Ransom, Nick Hook and more.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Crystal_Caines&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Crystal Caines is an Artist, Producer and Engineer. She began her production career in 2012. Caines went on to artist develop and produce for ASAP Ferg. In 2013, Caines produced the Bone Thugs-n-Harmony-featuring "Lord" on A$AP Ferg's debut album, Trap Lord, Continuing on to also engineering and premixing vocals for A$AP Ferg's future singles, "Work", "Shabba” and assisted in sonically shaping the majority of the TrapLord Album.<sup id="cite_ref-nadeska_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nadeska-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Caines draws her inspiration from artists like <a href="/wiki/Eve_(rapper)" title="Eve (rapper)">Eve</a> and producers like No ID.<sup id="cite_ref-nostro_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nostro-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2014, she released her debut single and Video, "Whiteline".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
Then went on to produce for other artist such as Bia, <a href="/wiki/Ro_James" title="Ro James">Ro James</a>, Johnny Cinco, <a href="/wiki/Smoke_DZA" title="Smoke DZA">DZA</a>, Abby Jasmine, Jack Harlow, Bryson Tiller, Leeky Bandz and more.
</p>
