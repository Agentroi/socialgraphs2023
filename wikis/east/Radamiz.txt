<p><b>Radhames Rodriguez</b> (born October 6, 1992), better known by his stage name <b>Radamiz</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> from <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_(state)" title="New York (state)">New York</a>. He is of <a href="/wiki/Dominican_Republic" title="Dominican Republic">Dominican</a> descent. Radamiz is a part of the Brooklyn-based collective, Mogul Club. In 2013, he was declared as <a href="/wiki/WQHT" title="WQHT">HOT 97</a>'s East Coast "Who's Next" artist and performed at <a href="/wiki/Summer_Jam_(festival)" title="Summer Jam (festival)">Summer Jam</a> on the festival stage later that year.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Radamiz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On April 5, 2016, Radamiz was announced as one of the performers at the 2016 <a href="/wiki/The_Brooklyn_Hip-Hop_Festival" title="The Brooklyn Hip-Hop Festival">Brooklyn Hip-Hop Festival</a> opening up for <a href="/wiki/Nas" title="Nas">Nas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Two weeks later he released his debut album, Writeous, which took four years to complete.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In October 2016, Radamiz was invited on <a href="/wiki/Statik_Selektah" title="Statik Selektah">Statik Selektah</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Shade_45" title="Shade 45">Shade 45</a> <a href="/wiki/Satellite_radio" title="Satellite radio">satellite radio</a> show "Showoff Radio" to promote his album.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In May 2018, Radamiz announced his signing to the relaunched <a href="/wiki/Payday_Records" title="Payday Records">Payday Records</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> That same month he released "V.I.M" produced by V'Don. Radamiz was one of the performers at the 2018 Brooklyn Hip-Hop Festival opening up for <a href="/wiki/Black_Star_(rap_duo)" title="Black Star (rap duo)">Black Star</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> On September 13, 2018, Radamiz was featured in a billboard and <a href="/wiki/New_York_City_Subway" title="New York City Subway">New York City Subway</a> ad campaign in New York City for <a href="/wiki/Depop" title="Depop">Depop</a>. Later that month he released a follow-up single titled "NYNYNYNY".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In January 2019, Radamiz was featured on a FifthGod song titled "Transgression" that included <a href="/wiki/Ab-Soul" title="Ab-Soul">Ab-Soul</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dash_(rapper)" title="Dash (rapper)">Da$H</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The following month he released his third single titled "Save The Youth" featuring History &amp; Tedy Andreas.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
