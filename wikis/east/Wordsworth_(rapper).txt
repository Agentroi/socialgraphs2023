<p><b>Vinson Jamel Johnson</b>, better known by his stage name <b>Wordsworth</b>, is an American rapper from <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Brooklyn, New York">Brooklyn, New York</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Wordsworth_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Wordsworth grew up in <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Brooklyn, New York">Brooklyn, New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He started rapping in fifth grade.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He graduated from the <a href="/wiki/State_University_of_New_York_at_Old_Westbury" title="State University of New York at Old Westbury">State University of New York at Old Westbury</a> and the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Miami" title="University of Miami">University of Miami</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Wordsworth recorded with his partner Punchline on <a href="/wiki/A_Tribe_Called_Quest" title="A Tribe Called Quest">A Tribe Called Quest</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/The_Love_Movement" title="The Love Movement">The Love Movement</a></i> and on <a href="/wiki/Black_Star_(rap_duo)" title="Black Star (rap duo)">Black Star</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Mos_Def_%26_Talib_Kweli_Are_Black_Star" title="Mos Def &amp; Talib Kweli Are Black Star">Mos Def &amp; Talib Kweli Are Black Star</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He was involved in the <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> comedy sketch series <i><a href="/wiki/The_Lyricist_Lounge_Show" title="The Lyricist Lounge Show">The Lyricist Lounge Show</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He also appeared in the Kevin Fitzgerald film <i><a href="/wiki/Freestyle:_The_Art_of_Rhyme" title="Freestyle: The Art of Rhyme">Freestyle: The Art of Rhyme</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> In 2004, he released his debut solo studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/Mirror_Music" title="Mirror Music">Mirror Music</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In 2012, he released another solo studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/The_Photo_Album_(Wordsworth_album)" title="The Photo Album (Wordsworth album)">The Photo Album</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
