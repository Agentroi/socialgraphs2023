<p><b>Myles Mills</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (born June 8, 1993),<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> better known by his stage name <b>Skizzy Mars</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a>, songwriter, and record producer from <a href="/wiki/Harlem,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Harlem, New York">Harlem, New York</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Diep_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Diep-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Skizzy_Mars&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mills was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Harlem,_New_York" class="mw-redirect" title="Harlem, New York">Harlem, New York</a> to his father, a therapist, and his mother, who operated two daycare centers in <a href="/wiki/Manhattan" title="Manhattan">Manhattan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Diep_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Diep-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He attended an all-boys school <a href="/wiki/St._Bernard%27s_School" title="St. Bernard&#39;s School">St. Bernard's School</a> during his elementary years and later graduated from the <a href="/wiki/Browning_School" title="Browning School">Browning School</a>. Mills attended <a href="/wiki/Union_College" title="Union College">Union College</a> to originally pursue a <a href="/wiki/Sports_journalism" title="Sports journalism">sports journalism</a> major,<sup id="cite_ref-Diep_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Diep-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> but he <a href="/wiki/Dropped_out" class="mw-redirect" title="Dropped out">dropped out</a> ten weeks after starting classes to begin his rapping career.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
