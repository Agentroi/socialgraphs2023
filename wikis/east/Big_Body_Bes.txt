Sadikay</b> (<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1176171273">.mw-parser-output .IPA-label-small{font-size:85%}.mw-parser-output .reference-text .IPA-label-small,.mw-parser-output .infobox .IPA-label-small,.mw-parser-output .navbox .IPA-label-small{font-size:100%}</style><span class="IPA-label IPA-label-small">Albanian pronunciation:</span> <span class="IPA nowrap" lang="sq-Latn-fonipa"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Albanian" title="Help:IPA/Albanian">&#91;ˈbɛsnik<span class="wrap"> </span>'sadikaj&#93;</a></span>; born August 26, 1984), better known by the <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Big Body Bes</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Television_presenter" title="Television presenter">TV presenter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is best known for his association with <a href="/wiki/Action_Bronson" title="Action Bronson">Action Bronson</a>, who describes Bes as his "Albanian cousin".<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He has been heavily featured on Action Bronson's musical and television projects.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Big_Body_Bes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bes was born in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> to <a href="/wiki/Albania" title="Albania">Albanian</a> parents who immigrated from southern Albania in 1983. He was raised in the quiet Lindenwood section of New York City. Bes met future collaborators Meyhem Lauren and Action Bronson at the age of 15.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Bes received the nickname "Big Body" due to his large stature.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Big_Body_Bes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Bes began his music career in 2011 at the age of 27, on Action Bronson's debut album <i>Dr. Lecter</i> with his forte of <a href="/wiki/Spoken_word" title="Spoken word">spoken word</a>, a strong composition of the elements of NYC street life, economic struggle and barbaric humor.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Bes rejects the notion that he's a "hypeman" or that his features on Bronson's songs are "skits", going as far as to say "...I wouldn’t even call them features, I would call them experiences". He considers his "breakout" appearance to be his feature on "9-24-11" off Bronson's mixtape Blue Chips.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Starting in 2016, Bes has also been on a TV show called <i><a href="/wiki/Traveling_the_Stars:_Action_Bronson_and_Friends_Watch_%27Ancient_Aliens%27" class="mw-redirect" title="Traveling the Stars: Action Bronson and Friends Watch &#39;Ancient Aliens&#39;">Traveling the Stars: Action Bronson and Friends Watch 'Ancient Aliens'</a></i>. Other hosts include Action Bronson, <a href="/wiki/Knxwledge" title="Knxwledge">Knxwledge</a>, <a href="/wiki/ScHoolboy_Q" class="mw-redirect" title="ScHoolboy Q">ScHoolboy Q</a>, <a href="/wiki/Earl_Sweatshirt" title="Earl Sweatshirt">Earl Sweatshirt</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tyler,_The_Creator" class="mw-redirect" title="Tyler, The Creator">Tyler, The Creator</a>, <a href="/wiki/Andy_Milonakis" title="Andy Milonakis">Andy Milonakis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Simon_Rex" title="Simon Rex">Dirt Nasty</a>, <a href="/wiki/MC_Eiht" title="MC Eiht">MC Eiht</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Alchemist_(musician)" title="The Alchemist (musician)">The Alchemist</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eric_Andr%C3%A9" title="Eric André">Eric André</a>, <a href="/wiki/Too_$hort" class="mw-redirect" title="Too $hort">Too $hort</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Melissa_Etheridge" title="Melissa Etheridge">Melissa Etheridge</a>. In the first episode of the series, Bes declares he doesn't believe in dinosaurs.
</p><p>Bes has stated that he was once known as Jerome, and has a cousin named Stacks. <sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
