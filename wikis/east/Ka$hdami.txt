<p><b>David Wallace III</b> (born December 16, 2004), known professionally as <b>Kashdami</b> (often stylized as <b>KA$HDAMI</b>), is an American rapper from Prince George’s County, Maryland. He rose to popularity with his songs "Reparations", "Look N The Mirror" and "14" with <a href="/wiki/Tana_(rapper)" title="Tana (rapper)">tana</a> which gained attraction on <a href="/wiki/SoundCloud" title="SoundCloud">SoundCloud</a> and <a href="/wiki/TikTok" title="TikTok">TikTok</a>. He released his first major label mixtape <i>Epiphany</i> in June 2021<sup id="cite_ref-Best_New_Artists_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Best_New_Artists-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ka$hdami&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>David Wallace III was born on December 16, 2004, in Las Vegas, Nevada, and was raised by his parents. He gained early experience with music through his father, who owned a studio and worked in the industry. He died when Wallace was seven. His family moved to <a href="/wiki/Maryland" title="Maryland">Maryland</a> around this time.<sup id="cite_ref-Best_New_Artists_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Best_New_Artists-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Wallace has a twin sister.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ka$hdami&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2018:_Beginnings">2018: Beginnings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ka$hdami&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2018: Beginnings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Wallace started making music at the age of eight through the now-defunct video chat app <a href="/wiki/OoVoo" title="OoVoo">ooVoo</a>. At thirteen, he bought a microphone and recorded on his mother's computer.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He released the song "Kappin Up" and <i>Findin' Out</i> in 2018 but became demotivated and took a break. Wallace returned when "Kappin Up" became popular on TikTok in 2020, with the help of a music video by <a href="/wiki/WorldStarHipHop" title="WorldStarHipHop">WorldStarHipHop</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-break_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-break-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="2020:_Breakthrough">2020: Breakthrough</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ka$hdami&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: 2020: Breakthrough">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>With newfound motivation, Wallace released the mixtapes #<i>KashDontMiss</i> and <i>16</i> in 2020, which grew his online fanbase. In January 2021, his breakout single "Reparations!" released, which gained popularity on various platforms including <a href="/wiki/TikTok" title="TikTok">TikTok</a> and <a href="/wiki/SoundCloud" title="SoundCloud">SoundCloud</a>. His follow-up single "Look N The Mirror!" did similarly well. Wallace signed a deal with <a href="/wiki/Republic_Records" title="Republic Records">Republic Records</a> in May 2021.<sup id="cite_ref-break_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-break-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="2021.E2.80.93present:_Epiphany.2C_HYPERNOVA.2C_WORLD_DAMINATION.2C_and_18"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="2021–present:_Epiphany,_HYPERNOVA,_WORLD_DAMINATION,_and_18">2021–present: <i>Epiphany</i>, <i>HYPERNOVA</i>, <i>WORLD DAMINATION</i>, and <i>18</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ka$hdami&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: 2021–present: Epiphany, HYPERNOVA, WORLD DAMINATION, and 18">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On June 2, 2021, the mixtape <i>Epiphany</i> released through Republic Records, with features from <a href="/wiki/Tana_(rapper)" title="Tana (rapper)">tana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yvngxchris" title="Yvngxchris">yvngxchris</a>, <a href="/wiki/SSGKobe" title="SSGKobe">SSGKobe</a>, Riovaz and D'mari Harris<sup id="cite_ref-Best_New_Artists_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Best_New_Artists-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Wallace and fellow rapper <a href="/wiki/Baby_Santana" class="mw-redirect" title="Baby Santana">tana</a>, formerly BabySantana, featured on the <a href="/wiki/Lyrical_Lemonade" class="mw-redirect" title="Lyrical Lemonade">Lyrical Lemonade</a> YouTube channel with their track "14". on July 20, 2021.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In September, Wallace released the single "Public", and followed it up with "Intermission" in October.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> They were the lead singles to his next mixtape <i>Hypernova</i>, it was released on November 12, 2021, with a sole feature from <a href="/wiki/Trippie_Redd" title="Trippie Redd">Trippie Redd</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>In March, Wallace released the single "posed2be" and followed it with "Phoenix" in April. He Later released "Famine" with XLOVCLO and then released the final single of his album WORLD DAMINATION with "komission" on September 9. A week later, WORLD DAMINATION was released on September 16, 2022. WORLD DAMINATION features the following artists: <a href="/wiki/Midwxst" title="Midwxst">midwxst</a>, XLOVCLO, NoCap, ilyfall and <a href="/wiki/Slump6s" title="Slump6s">Slump6s</a>. After had releasing a slew of singles off SoundCloud, on December 16, 2022, Wallace released an EP titled 18. <a href="/wiki/Matt_Ox" title="Matt Ox">Matt Ox</a> serves as the EP's sole feature.
</p>
