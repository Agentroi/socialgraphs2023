<p><b>Bernard Edwards Jr.</b> (born November 6, 1972),<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> professionally known as <b>Focus...</b>, is an American music producer from New York City. He gained major recognition in the music industry when he signed with <a href="/wiki/Dr._Dre" title="Dr. Dre">Dr. Dre</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Aftermath_Entertainment" title="Aftermath Entertainment">Aftermath Entertainment</a>, in 2002. Since then he has produced tracks for several prominent artists, including <a href="/wiki/Dr._Dre" title="Dr. Dre">Dr. Dre</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eminem" title="Eminem">Eminem</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kendrick_Lamar" title="Kendrick Lamar">Kendrick Lamar</a>, <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rick_Ross" title="Rick Ross">Rick Ross</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Legend" title="John Legend">John Legend</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Game_(rapper)" title="The Game (rapper)">The Game</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ice_Cube" title="Ice Cube">Ice Cube</a>, <a href="/wiki/Busta_Rhymes" title="Busta Rhymes">Busta Rhymes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fabolous" title="Fabolous">Fabolous</a>, <a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Schoolboy_Q" title="Schoolboy Q">Schoolboy Q</a>, <a href="/wiki/Joe_(singer)" title="Joe (singer)">Joe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christina_Aguilera" title="Christina Aguilera">Christina Aguilera</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beyonc%C3%A9_Knowles" class="mw-redirect" title="Beyoncé Knowles">Beyoncé</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lil_Wayne" title="Lil Wayne">Lil Wayne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Amerie" title="Amerie">Ameriie</a>, <a href="/wiki/Christina_Milian" title="Christina Milian">Christina Millian</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mac_Dre" title="Mac Dre">Mac Dre</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marsha_Ambrosius" title="Marsha Ambrosius">Marsha Ambrosius</a> among others.<sup id="cite_ref-a.Fam_MySpace_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-a.Fam_MySpace-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Focus...&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Focus... is the son of the late <a href="/wiki/Bernard_Edwards" title="Bernard Edwards">Bernard Edwards</a>, co-founder of <a href="/wiki/Disco" title="Disco">disco</a> and <a href="/wiki/R%26B" class="mw-redirect" title="R&amp;B">R&amp;B</a> band <a href="/wiki/Chic_(band)" title="Chic (band)">Chic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-a.Fam_MySpace_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-a.Fam_MySpace-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He was born in <a href="/wiki/Manhattan" title="Manhattan">Manhattan</a>, New York City, and raised in the <a href="/wiki/Tri-State_Region" class="mw-redirect" title="Tri-State Region">Tri-State Region</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-a.Fam_MySpace_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-a.Fam_MySpace-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Edwards Jr. credits his father with his love for the production side of music, which he developed from an early age, attending numerous studio sessions with his father.<sup id="cite_ref-a.Fam_MySpace_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-a.Fam_MySpace-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Focus...'s production talents were first recognized by Dr. Dre when an artist Edwards Jr. had produced, Daks, greatly impressed the Aftermath label head.<sup id="cite_ref-hitquarters.com_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hitquarters.com-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He later signed Focus... so that the two could then continue working together.<sup id="cite_ref-hitquarters.com_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hitquarters.com-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The beginning of 2009 saw Focus leave Aftermath after seven years with the label.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2014, Focus returned to Aftermath Entertainment as an official staff producer where he worked side by side with Dr. Dre and collaborators to help craft the Grammy nominated, highly anticipated album <i><a href="/wiki/Compton_(album)" title="Compton (album)">Compton</a></i>. <i>Compton</i> was <a href="/wiki/RIAA_certification" title="RIAA certification">certified gold</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_America" title="Recording Industry Association of America">Recording Industry Association of America</a> (RIAA).
</p><p>Focus is now the CEO of his own entertainment company N|VZXN Entertainment.
</p>
