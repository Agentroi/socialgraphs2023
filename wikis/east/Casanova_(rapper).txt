<p><b>Caswell Senior</b> (born October 27, 1986), known professionally as <b>Casanova</b>, is an American rapper. In 2016, Casanova made his first original song "Don't Run".<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is signed to <a href="/wiki/Roc_Nation" title="Roc Nation">Roc Nation</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In 2020, he was indicted on <a href="/wiki/Racketeer_Influenced_and_Corrupt_Organizations_Act" title="Racketeer Influenced and Corrupt Organizations Act">RICO</a> charges alongside 17 others due to various criminal activities which his <a href="/wiki/Bloods" title="Bloods">Bloods</a> gang "Untouchable Gorilla Stone Nation," allegedly participated in.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Casanova_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Caswell Senior was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, New York.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He is of <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a>, Haitian and Panamanian descent. At age 18, he was imprisoned on <a href="/wiki/Rikers_Island" title="Rikers Island">Rikers Island</a>, where he formed a close bond with his cellmate, who was later discovered to be rapper <a href="/wiki/ASAP_Rocky" title="ASAP Rocky">ASAP Rocky</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Casanova_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>While in a studio with friends in early 2016, Casanova, without previous experience, came up with "Don't Run". The song was produced by U-Dub (of NY Bangers).<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Upon its release "Don't Run" received heavy radio play from DJ Self on Power 105.1 in New York City,<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and later also <a href="/wiki/WWPR-FM" title="WWPR-FM">Power 105.1</a> and <a href="/wiki/WQHT" title="WQHT">Hot 97</a> there. In June 2016, Casanova released the official video for "Don't Run".<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Casanova was discovered by <a href="/wiki/Memphis_Bleek" title="Memphis Bleek">Memphis Bleek</a> through his label, Warehouse Music Group, which is under <a href="/wiki/Jay-Z" title="Jay-Z">Jay-Z</a>'s record label <a href="/wiki/Roc_Nation" title="Roc Nation">Roc Nation</a>. In October 2016, Casanova officially signed his deal with Warehouse/Roc Nation.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In September 2016, Casanova released the single "Line Me"<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> as well as a freestyle to <a href="/wiki/Mobb_Deep" title="Mobb Deep">Mobb Deep</a>'s "Quiet Storm".<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2017, Casanova released "Don't Run" Remix featuring <a href="/wiki/Young_M.A" title="Young M.A">Young M.A</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fabolous" title="Fabolous">Fabolous</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dave_East" title="Dave East">Dave East</a> &amp; <a href="/wiki/Don_Q_(rapper)" title="Don Q (rapper)">Don Q</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
He continued to put out music, releasing "The Old 50",<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> followed by his mixtape tape album  "Be Safe Tho"<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> featuring <a href="/wiki/Chris_Brown" title="Chris Brown">Chris Brown</a> and LO on the song "OHB".<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> On February 8, 2018, he released "Set Trippin" which addressed his beef with fellow New York artist <a href="/wiki/6ix9ine" title="6ix9ine">
Tekashi69</a>. He performed on the Party Tour with <a href="/wiki/Chris_Brown" title="Chris Brown">Chris Brown</a>, <a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a>, <a href="/wiki/French_Montana" title="French Montana">French Montana</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fabolous" title="Fabolous">Fabolous</a>, <a href="/wiki/O.T._Genasis" title="O.T. Genasis">O.T. Genasis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kap_G" title="Kap G">Kap G</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> On June 29, 2018, Casanova released his debut EP <i>Commissary</i> with guest appearances from <a href="/wiki/G-Eazy" title="G-Eazy">G-Eazy</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rich_the_Kid" title="Rich the Kid">Rich the Kid</a>, Snap Dogg, <a href="/wiki/A_Boogie_wit_da_Hoodie" title="A Boogie wit da Hoodie">A Boogie wit da Hoodie</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mozzy" title="Mozzy">Mozzy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Legal_issues">Legal issues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Casanova_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Legal issues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On December 1, 2020, prosecutors from the <a href="/wiki/United_States_District_Court_for_the_Southern_District_of_New_York" title="United States District Court for the Southern District of New York">Southern District of New York Federal Court</a> declared a warrant for the arrest of Casanova who they alleged was a leader within the Untouchable Gorilla Stone Nation Gang. He was among 18 alleged members of the Untouchable Gorilla Stone Nation Gang charged in connection with various racketeering, murder, narcotics, firearms and fraud offenses, officials said. The Acting U.S. Attorney <a href="/wiki/Audrey_Strauss" title="Audrey Strauss">Audrey Strauss</a> said: "As alleged in the Indictment, members of Gorilla Stone committed terrible acts of violence, trafficked in narcotics, and even engaged in brazen fraud by exploiting benefits programs meant to provide assistance in response to the COVID-19 pandemic". The FBI apprehended 17 of the gang members, but Casanova was not detained and an arrest warrant was issued for him.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> On December 2, 2020, Casanova surrendered to federal authorities.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> On June 27, 2023, he was sentenced to 188 months (15 years, 8 months) in prison.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>In 2016, Casanova started his own company called <style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1023754711">.mw-parser-output .vanchor>:target~.vanchor-text{background-color:#b1d2ff}</style><span class="vanchor"><span id="2x_Entertainment"></span><span class="vanchor-text">2x Entertainment</span></span>. </p>
