<p><b>Gabriel Stevenson</b>, professionally known as <b>Like</b>, is an American record producer, rapper, DJ and songwriter. A three time <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Nominees" title="Grammy Nominees">Grammy nominee</a>, he began working with the <a href="/wiki/Pac_Div" title="Pac Div">Pacific Division</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop" class="mw-redirect" title="Hip hop">hip hop</a> trio, better known as Pac Div in 2006.
</p><p>In 2012, he produced <a href="/wiki/Kendrick_Lamar" title="Kendrick Lamar">Kendrick Lamar</a>'s "Sing About Me" on <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Kid,_M.A.A.D_City" title="Good Kid, M.A.A.D City">Good Kid, M.A.A.D City</a></i>. He received two Grammy Award nominations at the <a href="/wiki/56th_Grammy_Awards" class="mw-redirect" title="56th Grammy Awards">56th Grammy Awards</a>: <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Album_of_the_Year" class="mw-redirect" title="Grammy Album of the Year">Album of the Year</a> and <a href="/wiki/Best_Rap_Album" class="mw-redirect" title="Best Rap Album">Best Rap Album</a>. In 2016, he produced <a href="/wiki/Anderson_Paak" class="mw-redirect" title="Anderson Paak">Anderson .Paak</a>'s "Room in Here" featuring <a href="/wiki/The_Game_(rapper)" title="The Game (rapper)">The Game</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sonyae_Elise" title="Sonyae Elise">Sonyae Elise</a> on the <i><a href="/wiki/Malibu_(album)" title="Malibu (album)">Malibu</a></i> album.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The album received a Grammy nomination for <a href="/wiki/Best_Urban_Contemporary_Album" class="mw-redirect" title="Best Urban Contemporary Album">Best Urban Contemporary Album</a> at the <a href="/wiki/59th_Annual_Grammy_Awards" title="59th Annual Grammy Awards">59th Annual Grammy Awards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Like has production credit with artists such as <a href="/wiki/Mac_Miller" title="Mac Miller">Mac Miller</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ab-Soul" title="Ab-Soul">Ab-Soul</a>, <a href="/wiki/Joey_Badass" title="Joey Badass">Joey Badass</a>, Nick Grant, <a href="/wiki/Asher_Roth" title="Asher Roth">Asher Roth</a>, Ill Camille and <a href="/wiki/Currensy" title="Currensy">Currensy</a>.
</p><p>Like has released music as a solo artist.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He released instrumental albums, <i>Zesty</i> and <i>Lightwork</i> in 2014 and <i>Emeralds</i><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> in 2016. On September 30, 2016, he released his first solo rap album, <i>Songs Made While High</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> which features Anderson .Paak, Buddy, and <a href="/wiki/Kali_Uchis" title="Kali Uchis">Kali Uchis</a>.
</p><p>In 2017, Pac Div regrouped and are scheduled to release an album at the end of the year.
</p>
