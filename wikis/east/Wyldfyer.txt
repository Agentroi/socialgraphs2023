<p><b>Wyatt Coleman</b>, also known as <b>Wyldfyer</b> is an American music producer. He produced a promo album (from <a href="/wiki/Jay-Z" title="Jay-Z">Jay-Z</a>), The Black Album: Classik. He produced tracks for <a href="/wiki/Nas" title="Nas">Nas</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Hip_Hop_Is_Dead" title="Hip Hop Is Dead">Hip Hop Is Dead</a>", and the collaboration between <a href="/wiki/Nas" title="Nas">Nas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jay-Z" title="Jay-Z">Jay-Z</a>,  "<a href="/wiki/Black_Republican_(song)" class="mw-redirect" title="Black Republican (song)">Black Republican</a>".
</p><p>After producing the Nas hit "War is Necessary" which appears in the video game, <a href="/wiki/Grand_Theft_Auto_IV" title="Grand Theft Auto IV">Grand Theft Auto IV</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-gta4_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gta4-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Wyldfyer collaborated with Ludacris and <a href="/wiki/Lil_Wayne" title="Lil Wayne">Lil Wayne</a>.
</p>
