<p><b>Sade Lamiere Davis</b> known as Lamiere is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> from <a href="/wiki/The_Bronx" title="The Bronx">The Bronx, New York</a> born in year <a href="/wiki/1990" title="1990">1990</a>. Her first notable appearance was on <a href="/wiki/Snapchat" title="Snapchat">Snapchat</a> reality series "Second Chance" which aired in December of 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_Career">Life and Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lamiere&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sade Lamiere Davis professionally known as Lamiere was born September 8th 1990 and  is an African American Bronx native raised by a single mother in NYC's Housing projects.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  Lamiere was a high performing student and graduated the 8th grade as the salutatorian of her class.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  Lamiere began rapping at the age of 16 and was influenced by her mother who was a rapper in the 1990's. However she did not see rapping as more than a hobby until age 23 during her first visit to a professional <a href="/wiki/Recording_studio" title="Recording studio">recording studio</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>  Lamiere was also influenced to persue rapping, after experiencing racism within corporate America<sup id="cite_ref-:0_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Lamiere took on many freelance modeling and background acting gigs to make money for studio time. Lamiere had been featured in the <a href="/wiki/Jadakiss" title="Jadakiss">Jadakiss</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rick_Ross" title="Rick Ross">Rick Ross</a> music video "Kiss the Sky," as well a <a href="/wiki/Mr._Cheeks" title="Mr. Cheeks">Mr. Cheeks</a> "New York City" music video. Lamiere debuted her first single "Lone Ranger" in February of 2020. Lamiere is presently an independent artist and writes, records and mixes her own music.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_style">Musical style</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lamiere&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Musical style">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lamiere is a <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">Hip-hop</a> artist. She releasing music in many different sub-genres of Hiphop including <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn_drill" title="Brooklyn drill">New York Drill</a>, melodic, dance and <a href="/wiki/Twerking" title="Twerking">twerk.</a><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2023)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Influences">Influences</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lamiere&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Influences">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Lamiere has noted several musical influences including <a href="/wiki/Nicki_Minaj" title="Nicki Minaj">Nicki Minaj</a>, <a href="/wiki/Remy_Ma" title="Remy Ma">Remy Ma</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jay-Z" title="Jay-Z">JayZ</a>, <a href="/wiki/Beyonc%C3%A9" title="BeyoncÃ©">Beyonce</a>, <a href="/wiki/Drake_(musician)" title="Drake (musician)">Drake</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Notorious_B.I.G." title="The Notorious B.I.G.">Biggie Smalls</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Gates" title="Kevin Gates">Kevin Gates</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lil%27_Kim" title="Lil&#39; Kim">Lil Kim</a>. She stated that she frequently listens to music from <a href="/wiki/Doja_Cat" title="Doja Cat">Doja Cat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Megan_Thee_Stallion" title="Megan Thee Stallion">Megan the Stallion</a>, <a href="/wiki/Stunna_Girl" title="Stunna Girl">Stunna Girl</a> ,<a href="/wiki/Lakeyah" title="Lakeyah">Lakeyah</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lola_Brooke" title="Lola Brooke">Lola Brooke</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ice_Spice" title="Ice Spice">Ice Spice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cardi_B" title="Cardi B">Cardi B</a>. 
</p>
