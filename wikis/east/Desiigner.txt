<p><b>Sidney Royel Selby III</b> (born May 3, 1997), known professionally as <b>Desiigner</b>, is an American rapper, singer, and convicted sex offender. He is best known for his December 2015 debut single "<a href="/wiki/Panda_(Desiigner_song)" class="mw-redirect" title="Panda (Desiigner song)">Panda</a>", which topped the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>. Shortly after the song gained viral status online, he signed to <a href="/wiki/Kanye_West" title="Kanye West">Kanye West</a>'s <a href="/wiki/GOOD_Music" title="GOOD Music">GOOD Music</a>, an imprint of <a href="/wiki/Def_Jam_Recordings" title="Def Jam Recordings">Def Jam Recordings</a> until becoming an independent artist in 2019.
</p><p>In July 2016, he released the follow-up single "<a href="/wiki/Tiimmy_Turner" title="Tiimmy Turner">Tiimmy Turner</a>", which peaked at number 34 on the <i>Billboard</i> Hot 100. Despite this, Desiigner is frequently described as a <a href="/wiki/One-hit_wonder" title="One-hit wonder">one-hit wonder</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desiigner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Desiigner was born on May 3, 1997,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, New York City.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> He is of <a href="/wiki/Afro-Barbadian" class="mw-redirect" title="Afro-Barbadian">Afro-Barbadian</a> and <a href="/wiki/African_American" class="mw-redirect" title="African American">African American</a> descent,<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> and is the grandson of blues musician <a href="/wiki/Guitar_Crusher" title="Guitar Crusher">Sidney "Guitar Crusher" Selby</a>. He grew up in the <a href="/wiki/Louis_Armstrong" title="Louis Armstrong">Louis Armstrong</a> housing projects in the <a href="/wiki/Bedford%E2%80%93Stuyvesant,_Brooklyn" title="Bedford–Stuyvesant, Brooklyn">Bedford–Stuyvesant</a> neighborhood.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He began practicing vocals at school choir and church. At the age of 14, he embarked on his musical career.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desiigner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Desiigner embarked on his music career under his original <a href="/wiki/Nickname" title="Nickname">nickname</a> Dezolo,<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> before he began releasing his work under the alias of Designer Royel, especially with Royel (his middle name). His sister convinced him to drop the "Royel" part and then keep the "Designer" part as his new <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a>, doubling the "I"<span class="nowrap" style="padding-left:0.1em;">&#39;s</span>. On December 3, 2015, under his new <a href="/wiki/Pseudonym" title="Pseudonym">pseudonym</a>, Desiigner released his debut track, called "Zombie Walk". The song was <a href="/wiki/Record_production" class="mw-redirect" title="Record production">produced</a> by The Shell God.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> On December 15, 2015, Desiigner released his commercial debut <a href="/wiki/Single_(music)" title="Single (music)">single</a>, titled "<a href="/wiki/Panda_(Desiigner_song)" class="mw-redirect" title="Panda (Desiigner song)">Panda</a>" on SoundCloud.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> The song released on <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a> five days later, before being re-released as of February 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> On February 11, 2016, Desiigner signed a <a href="/wiki/Recording_contract" title="Recording contract">recording contract</a> with <a href="/wiki/Kanye_West" title="Kanye West">Kanye West</a>'s <a href="/wiki/GOOD_Music" title="GOOD Music">GOOD Music</a> imprint, under the aegis of <a href="/wiki/Def_Jam_Recordings" title="Def Jam Recordings">Def Jam Recordings</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> After signing a record deal, Desiigner subsequently appeared on two tracks — "<a href="/wiki/Pt._2" class="mw-redirect" title="Pt. 2">Pt. 2</a>" (which features an <a href="/wiki/Interpolation_(popular_music)" title="Interpolation (popular music)">interpolation</a> of "Panda") and "Freestyle 4" — that were taken from Kanye West's seventh album <i><a href="/wiki/The_Life_of_Pablo" title="The Life of Pablo">The Life of Pablo</a></i>. Desiigner then went on to perform at the 2016's <a href="/wiki/South_by_Southwest" title="South by Southwest">South by Southwest</a> (SXSW) music festival.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> In an interview with <i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i>, Desiigner confirmed the release of his first upcoming <a href="/wiki/Mixtape" title="Mixtape">mixtape</a>, titled <i>Trap History Month</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-bbinterview_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbinterview-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On May 5, 2016, <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">producer</a> <a href="/wiki/Mike_Dean_(record_producer)" title="Mike Dean (record producer)">Mike Dean</a> announced that he would be serving as the <a href="/wiki/Executive_producer#Music" title="Executive producer">executive producer</a> for Desiigner's upcoming debut studio album.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> On May 24, Desiigner announced that the title to his upcoming debut album would be called <i>The Life of Desiigner</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> In June 2016, <a href="/wiki/XXL_(magazine)" title="XXL (magazine)"><i>XXL</i> magazine</a> revealed their annual "Freshmen Class" for 2016, and Desiigner was included on the list with nine other new artists.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> In June 2016, "<a href="/wiki/Champions_(GOOD_Music_song)" title="Champions (GOOD Music song)">Champions</a>" was released as the lead single from the GOOD Music's upcoming compilation album, <i>Cruel Winter</i>. Desiigner made a <a href="/wiki/Guest_appearance" title="Guest appearance">guest appearance</a> on this track, alongside his label-mate Kanye West, and other <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rappers</a> such as <a href="/wiki/Gucci_Mane" title="Gucci Mane">Gucci Mane</a>, <a href="/wiki/Big_Sean" title="Big Sean">Big Sean</a>, <a href="/wiki/2_Chainz" title="2 Chainz">2 Chainz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Travis_Scott" title="Travis Scott">Travis Scott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yo_Gotti" title="Yo Gotti">Yo Gotti</a> and <a href="/wiki/Migos" title="Migos">Quavo</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> In the same month, Desiigner made his US television debut, when he performed his single, "Panda" at the <a href="/wiki/BET_Awards_2016" title="BET Awards 2016">2016's BET Awards</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> On June 26, 2016, Desiigner released his first full-length mixtape, titled <i><a href="/wiki/New_English_(mixtape)" title="New English (mixtape)">New English</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/Tidal_(service)" title="Tidal (service)">Tidal</a>, following a live premiere of the mixtape on June 22.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> In July 2016, American pop singer <a href="/wiki/Demi_Lovato" title="Demi Lovato">Demi Lovato</a> brought out Desiigner at their concert in New York.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> On July 21, 2016, Desiigner premiered the track, called "<a href="/wiki/Tiimmy_Turner" title="Tiimmy Turner">Tiimmy Turner</a>", which was released as a single on iTunes the next day.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Desiigner appeared as a special guest on the&#160;<a href="/wiki/Netflix" title="Netflix">Netflix</a>&#160;series&#160;<i><a href="/wiki/Bill_Nye_Saves_the_World" title="Bill Nye Saves the World">Bill Nye Saves the World</a></i>. On November 7, 2017, it was reported that <a href="/wiki/BTS_(band)" class="mw-redirect" title="BTS (band)">BTS</a> would release a remix of their song "<a href="/wiki/Mic_Drop_(song)" title="Mic Drop (song)">Mic Drop</a>" featuring Desiigner and remixed by <a href="/wiki/Steve_Aoki" title="Steve Aoki">Steve Aoki</a>. It was set to be released on November 17, 2017, but was postponed and released on November 24, 2017, instead.
</p>

<p>On May 3, 2018, Desiigner announced via social media that his debut EP, <i><a href="/wiki/L.O.D._(EP)" title="L.O.D. (EP)">L.O.D.</a></i>, would be released at midnight on May 4, 2018.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2020, Desiigner released "Diva" under his independent label LOD Records.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In a 2022 interview with <a href="/wiki/VladTV" class="mw-redirect" title="VladTV">VladTV</a>, Desiigner explained why he left GOOD Music in 2019, saying he was "going through a little mental thing" including his father having been comatose, and had tried to contact Kanye and his other labelmates for guidance but didn't receive any.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the morning of November 1, 2022, Desiigner went to Instagram Live saying "I'm done rapping," after hearing the news of <a href="/wiki/Migos" title="Migos">Migos</a> rapper <a href="/wiki/Takeoff_(rapper)" title="Takeoff (rapper)">Takeoff</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Death_of_Takeoff" class="mw-redirect" title="Death of Takeoff">death from a shooting in Houston</a>, Texas that same morning.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> On November 26, he returned to rap, releasing a track and a video.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Legal_issues">Legal issues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desiigner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Legal issues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On September 8, 2016, Desiigner and three other people were arrested in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, after a 911 caller claimed that he pulled a gun on them. Desiigner was later found in an <a href="/wiki/SUV" title="SUV">SUV</a> with four other people and the vehicle was searched. The police allegedly found <a href="/wiki/Oxycodone" title="Oxycodone">oxycodone</a> and guns, for which he and the other individuals received drug and weapon charges. They were all detained in custody.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> On September 10, 2016, his felony <a href="/wiki/Gun_charge" class="mw-redirect" title="Gun charge">gun charge</a> was dropped by prosecutors.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> He was cleared of the charges on September 12, 2016, when it was found that the pills were <a href="/wiki/Anabolic_steroids" class="mw-redirect" title="Anabolic steroids">anabolic steroids</a> belonging to his driver.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2023, Desiigner was charged with indecent exposure after allegedly being caught on a <a href="/wiki/Delta_Air_Lines" title="Delta Air Lines">Delta Air Lines</a> flight <a href="/wiki/Masturbation" title="Masturbation">masturbating</a> in front of <a href="/wiki/Flight_attendant" title="Flight attendant">flight attendants</a> while travelling from <a href="/wiki/Tokyo" title="Tokyo">Tokyo</a> to <a href="/wiki/Minneapolis" title="Minneapolis">Minneapolis</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_style">Musical style</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Desiigner&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Musical style">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Desiigner's musical style is influenced by <a href="/wiki/Southern_hip_hop" title="Southern hip hop">Southern hip hop</a>, namely <a href="/wiki/Trap_music_(hip_hop)" class="mw-redirect" title="Trap music (hip hop)">trap music</a> which originated in <a href="/wiki/Atlanta,_Georgia" class="mw-redirect" title="Atlanta, Georgia">Atlanta, Georgia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bbinterview_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbinterview-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> His <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapping</a> technique and vocals have been heavily compared to that of Atlanta-based rapper <a href="/wiki/Future_(rapper)" title="Future (rapper)">Future</a>. When asked about the frequent comparisons, he told <i><a href="/wiki/Complex_(magazine)" class="mw-redirect" title="Complex (magazine)">Complex</a></i>: "God gave him a blessing, but he gave me a blessing too. I ain't gonna doubt the man's music. He makes beautiful music too. Music is made every day. Big ups to him, big ups to Future. I actually like Future's music. I like his music, you feel me. I'm not a hater or a critic on him, you know, I do me. God bless him, God bless me."<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
