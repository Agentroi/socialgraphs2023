<p><b>Anthony Best</b> (born March 20,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> 1968)<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> professionally known as <b>Buckwild</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop</a> producer.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Hailing from <a href="/wiki/The_Bronx" title="The Bronx">The Bronx</a> borough of <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>, he is a member of <a href="/wiki/Diggin%27_in_the_Crates_Crew" class="mw-redirect" title="Diggin&#39; in the Crates Crew">Diggin' in the Crates Crew</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Lord_Finesse" title="Lord Finesse">Lord Finesse</a>, <a href="/wiki/Showbiz_and_A.G." title="Showbiz and A.G.">Showbiz and A.G.</a>, <a href="/wiki/Diamond_D" title="Diamond D">Diamond D</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fat_Joe" title="Fat Joe">Fat Joe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Big_L_(rapper)" class="mw-redirect" title="Big L (rapper)">Big L</a>, and <a href="/wiki/O.C._(rapper)" title="O.C. (rapper)">O.C.</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He has produced a number of tracks for prominent rappers in the music industry, including <a href="/wiki/The_Notorious_B.I.G." title="The Notorious B.I.G.">The Notorious B.I.G.</a>'s "I Got a Story to Tell" and <a href="/wiki/Black_Rob" title="Black Rob">Black Rob</a>'s "<a href="/wiki/Whoa!_(Black_Rob_song)" title="Whoa! (Black Rob song)">Whoa!</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In 2013, he was described by <i><a href="/wiki/HipHopDX" title="HipHopDX">HipHopDX</a></i> as "one of Hip Hop's most prolific and acclaimed producers".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
