<p><b>Thomas Lee Brown</b> (born May 1, 1986), also known as <b>TBHits</b>, is an American record producer, songwriter and rapper. Brown currently lives in LA and was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Pittsburgh,_Pennsylvania" class="mw-redirect" title="Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania">Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania</a>. He is an <a href="/wiki/ASCAP" class="mw-redirect" title="ASCAP">ASCAP</a> award winner and a <a href="/wiki/Grammy" class="mw-redirect" title="Grammy">Grammy</a> nominee. He has scored multiple chart topping albums while working with <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Grande" title="Ariana Grande">Ariana Grande</a> on all six of her studio albums. Brown has also collected production credits for three songs on Grammy-award-winning artist <a href="/wiki/Meghan_Trainor" title="Meghan Trainor">Meghan Trainor</a>'s second album, <i><a href="/wiki/Thank_You_(Meghan_Trainor_album)" title="Thank You (Meghan Trainor album)">Thank You</a></i> (2016).
</p><p>Brown learned from production greats <a href="/wiki/Roy_%22Royalty%22_Hamilton" title="Roy &quot;Royalty&quot; Hamilton">Roy "Royalty" Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rodney_Jerkins" title="Rodney Jerkins">Rodney Jerkins</a>, while working alongside them and eventually creating a team of his own.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Producing_and_songwriting">Producing and songwriting</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Tommy_Brown_(record_producer)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Producing and songwriting">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Before working in music, Brown worked at <a href="/wiki/Sears" title="Sears">Sears</a> in <a href="/wiki/Atlanta,_Georgia" class="mw-redirect" title="Atlanta, Georgia">Atlanta, Georgia</a>. Every night, he would hand out around 50 CDs with his songs on them and snippets of <a href="/wiki/The_Notorious_B.I.G." title="The Notorious B.I.G.">The Notorious B.I.G.</a>'s "Ten Crack Commandments" over the tracks. He was finally contacted by <a href="/wiki/Gorilla_Zoe" title="Gorilla Zoe">Gorilla Zoe</a> who invited Brown to work in the studio with him.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Originally, starting in 2008, Brown worked with production group Darkchild in his early career. During this time, he worked with artists such as <a href="/wiki/Jennifer_Lopez" title="Jennifer Lopez">Jennifer Lopez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wyclef_Jean" title="Wyclef Jean">Wyclef Jean</a>. He has also worked with <a href="/wiki/The_Black_Eyed_Peas" class="mw-redirect" title="The Black Eyed Peas">The Black Eyed Peas</a>, most notably their song "<a href="/wiki/Just_Can%27t_Get_Enough_(The_Black_Eyed_Peas_song)" class="mw-redirect" title="Just Can&#39;t Get Enough (The Black Eyed Peas song)">Just Can't Get Enough</a>".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> In 2012, Brown became independent with publishing through <a href="/wiki/Sony/ATV_Music_Publishing" class="mw-redirect" title="Sony/ATV Music Publishing">Sony/ATV Music Publishing</a>. He lives in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, producing tracks for established stars and new acts like <a href="/wiki/Meek_Mill" title="Meek Mill">Meek Mill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Travis_Scott_(rapper)" class="mw-redirect" title="Travis Scott (rapper)">Travis Scott</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cee_Lo_Green" class="mw-redirect" title="Cee Lo Green">Cee Lo Green</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nas" title="Nas">Nas</a>, <a href="/wiki/T.I." title="T.I.">T.I.</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ariana_Grande" title="Ariana Grande">Ariana Grande</a>, <a href="/wiki/John_Legend" title="John Legend">John Legend</a>, <a href="/wiki/Teyana_Taylor" title="Teyana Taylor">Teyana Taylor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Cyhi_the_Prynce" title="Cyhi the Prynce">Cyhi the Prynce</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victoria_Mon%C3%A9t" title="Victoria Monét">Victoria Monét</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mario_(American_singer)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mario (American singer)">Mario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Chrisette_Michele" title="Chrisette Michele">Chrisette Michele</a>, Kelly Rowland, <a href="/wiki/Twice" title="Twice">Twice</a> and <a href="/wiki/2_Chainz" title="2 Chainz">2 Chainz</a>.
</p>
