<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Hell_Rell&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

The album reached No. 5 on both the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)"><i>Billboard</i></a> Top Independent Albums and Top Rap Albums chart, and No. 10 on the Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
