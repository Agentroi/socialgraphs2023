<p><b>Scott Keeney</b> (born 15 November 1983), better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>DJ Skee</b>, is an American artist, television host, radio personality, and entrepreneur. Skee rose to fame as the first DJ to discover and play superstar artists on the radio including <a href="/wiki/Kendrick_Lamar" title="Kendrick Lamar">Kendrick Lamar</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Justin_Bieber" title="Justin Bieber">Justin Bieber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Akon" title="Akon">Akon</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lorde" title="Lorde">Lorde</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Lady_Gaga" title="Lady Gaga">Lady Gaga</a> amongst many others.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Radio_career">Radio career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=DJ_Skee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Radio career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2015, Skee officially launched <a href="/wiki/Dash_Radio" title="Dash Radio">Dash Radio</a>, which has since become world's largest all original digital radio network with investments from the likes of <a href="/wiki/L.A._Reid" title="L.A. Reid">L.A. Reid</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Peterson" title="Adrian Peterson">Adrian Peterson</a>, Kevin Colleran, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Lazerow" title="Michael Lazerow">Michael Lazerow</a> and more. Now reaching over 5 million listeners worldwide, Dash Radio powers over 75 stations completely free and curated by real DJs, artists and tastemakers with zero traditional commercials.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Dash Radio has partnered with the likes of <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kylie_Jenner" title="Kylie Jenner">Kylie Jenner</a>, <a href="/wiki/Odd_Future" title="Odd Future">Odd Future</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=The_GRAMMYs&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="The GRAMMYs (page does not exist)">The GRAMMYs</a>, <a href="/wiki/EA_Sports" title="EA Sports">EA Sports</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tionne_Watkins" title="Tionne Watkins">T-Boz</a> (<a href="/wiki/TLC_(group)" title="TLC (group)">TLC</a>), <a href="/wiki/Stevie_Wonder" title="Stevie Wonder">Stevie Wonder</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wu-Tang_Clan" title="Wu-Tang Clan">Wu-Tang Clan</a>, <a href="/wiki/XXL_Magazine" class="mw-redirect" title="XXL Magazine">XXL Magazine</a>, Strange Music Inc., The Computer Game Show, multiple music festivals, and many more.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Business_career">Business career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=DJ_Skee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Business career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Outside of his career as a DJ and TV personality, Skee is active as an entrepreneur being named one of <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Magazine" class="mw-redirect" title="Billboard Magazine">Billboard Magazine</a>'s influential "Power Players: 30 Under 30"<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> in the 21 August 2010 issue. Skee was also named as an honorable mention <i><a href="/wiki/Forbes" title="Forbes">Forbes</a></i> magazine's influential "Power Players: 30 Under 30"<sup id="cite_ref-forbes30under30_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-forbes30under30-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> in 2012. In September 2012, Skee opened the second and flagship location of his retail store chain, Tradition, in the <a href="/wiki/Beverly_Center" title="Beverly Center">Beverly Center</a> in <a href="/wiki/Beverly_Hills" class="mw-redirect" title="Beverly Hills">Beverly Hills</a>, CA.
</p><p>Known as the man behind the marketing campaigns and promotions for the popular <a href="/wiki/T-Mobile_Sidekick" class="mw-redirect" title="T-Mobile Sidekick">T-Mobile Sidekick</a> in the mid-2000s, Skee has orchestrated campaigns for <a href="/wiki/Daimler-Chrysler" class="mw-redirect" title="Daimler-Chrysler">Daimler-Chrysler</a> (and specifically the launch of the <a href="/wiki/Chrysler_300" title="Chrysler 300">Chrysler 300</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dodge_Magnum" title="Dodge Magnum">Dodge Magnum</a> in 2004), Google's Android Platform, <a href="/wiki/Nike_Inc" class="mw-redirect" title="Nike Inc">Nike</a>, nearly every major record label in the world, and more.  Additionally, he has written pieces for magazines such as Forbes, Billboard, Recode<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In February, 2021 DJ Skee partnered with <a href="/wiki/Topps" title="Topps">Topps</a> for its Project 70 baseball card collection.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>  As a mixtape DJ, Skee created curated playlists for each of his cards on <a href="/wiki/Spotify" title="Spotify">Spotify</a>.
</p><p>On May 24, 2022, DJ Skee released the book "The Metaverse Handbook". Co-authored with Quharrison Terry with a foreword written by <a href="/wiki/Paris_Hilton" title="Paris Hilton">Paris Hilton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="DJ_career">DJ career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=DJ_Skee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: DJ career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>As a mixtape DJ &amp; producer, Skee has released over 100 mixtapes during his career. In 2008, Skee was nominated in eight categories, twice as much as any other DJ, at Justo's Mixtape Awards.<sup id="cite_ref-nominations_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nominations-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Skee also won west coast mixtape DJ of the year at the 2013 Global Spin Awards.
</p><p>Skee provided the sonic and musical identity for the 2012 <a href="/wiki/Dew_Tour" title="Dew Tour">Dew Tour</a> in conjunction with <a href="/wiki/NBC" title="NBC">NBC</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mountain_Dew" title="Mountain Dew">Mountain Dew</a> as well as the athletes on the tour.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was announced in 2012 that Skee would be involved with the music for <i><a href="/wiki/Halo_4" title="Halo 4">Halo 4</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/Microsoft" title="Microsoft">Microsoft</a> <a href="/wiki/Xbox_360" title="Xbox 360">Xbox 360</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On November 27, 2021, DJ Skee was the guest DJ for the <a href="/wiki/NCAA" class="mw-redirect" title="NCAA">NCAA</a> football game in <a href="/wiki/Michigan" title="Michigan">Michigan</a> between The <a href="/wiki/Michigan_Wolverines_football" title="Michigan Wolverines football">Michigan Wolverines football</a> team and The <a href="/wiki/Ohio_State_Buckeyes_football" title="Ohio State Buckeyes football">Ohio State Buckeyes football</a> team.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Skee will return to <a href="/wiki/Michigan_Stadium" title="Michigan Stadium">Michigan Stadium</a> for Michigan's game versus the <a href="/wiki/Penn_State_Nittany_Lions_football" title="Penn State Nittany Lions football">Penn State Nittany Lions football</a> team on October 15, 2022.
</p><p>On February 6, 2022 DJ Skee became the first DJ to DJ live during a <a href="/wiki/NASCAR" title="NASCAR">NASCAR</a> race for the <a href="/wiki/Busch_Light_Clash_at_The_Coliseum" title="Busch Light Clash at The Coliseum">Busch Light Clash at The Coliseum</a> in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Television">Television</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=DJ_Skee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Television">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Now in its 5th season on Fuse, "Skee TV" is a one-hour original series that provides insider access to the hottest musical artists, athletes, celebrities and cultural tastemakers via exclusive interviews and live performances.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On June 21, 2021, DJ Skee threw out the first pitch at <a href="/wiki/Petco_Park" title="Petco Park">Petco Park</a> before the game between the <a href="/wiki/Padres" class="mw-redirect" title="Padres">Padres</a> vs <a href="/wiki/Dodgers" class="mw-redirect" title="Dodgers">Dodgers</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On July 15, 2021, <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Magazine" class="mw-redirect" title="Billboard Magazine">Billboard Magazine</a> released an article announcing that a documentary on the death of <a href="/wiki/Eazy-E" title="Eazy-E">Eazy-E</a> would be airing on <a href="/wiki/MeTV" title="MeTV">MeTV</a> with DJ Skee as one of the Executive Producers.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
