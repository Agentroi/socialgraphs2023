<p><b>Troy Ryan</b> (born October 25, 1986), better known by his stage name <b>DJ Webstar</b> (or simply <b>Webstar</b>) is an American DJ and <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">record producer</a>. In 2006, his song "<a href="/wiki/Chicken_Noodle_Soup_(Webstar_song)" class="mw-redirect" title="Chicken Noodle Soup (Webstar song)">Chicken Noodle Soup</a>", became popular and became an <a href="/wiki/Internet_meme" title="Internet meme">Internet meme</a>, with fans uploading amateur videos dancing to the track. The song's popularity led to him being signed by the now defunct <a href="/wiki/Universal_Records_(defunct_record_label)" class="mw-redirect" title="Universal Records (defunct record label)">Universal Records</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
