<p><b>Michael Jean Clervoix</b><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (born February 11, 1976), professionally known as <b>Sha Money XL</b>, is an American DJ, songwriter, record producer, and music manager from <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sha_Money_XL&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Clervoix was a producer for <a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a> and the founder of Teamwork Music Inc.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> After <a href="/wiki/Interscope_Records" title="Interscope Records">Interscope</a> granted 50 Cent his own label in 2003, Sha Money XL was president of <a href="/wiki/G-Unit_Records" title="G-Unit Records">G-Unit Records</a> until 2007. He then was VP of <a href="/wiki/Artists_and_repertoire" title="Artists and repertoire">A&amp;R</a> at <a href="/wiki/Def_Jam_Recordings" title="Def Jam Recordings">Def Jam</a> in May 2010, and produced <a href="/wiki/Big_K.R.I.T." title="Big K.R.I.T.">Big K.R.I.T.</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Live_from_the_Underground" title="Live from the Underground">Live from the Underground</a></i>. As of 2015<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Sha_Money_XL&amp;action=edit">&#91;update&#93;</a></sup> he is an executive at <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records" title="Epic Records">Epic Records</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sha_Money_XL&amp;action=edit&amp;section=22" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Clervoix who is of <a href="/wiki/Haiti" title="Haiti">Haitian</a> descent, joined in on the relief efforts a year later in the aftermath of the <a href="/wiki/2010_Haiti_earthquake" title="2010 Haiti earthquake">2010 Haiti earthquake</a> that still left many people impoverished and homeless sending over money, food and clothes to <a href="/wiki/Port-au-Prince" title="Port-au-Prince">Port-au-Prince</a>, as well as his mother’s hometown of <a href="/wiki/Limb%C3%A9,_Nord" class="mw-redirect" title="Limbé, Nord">Limbé</a>. He sought to create a Haitian hip-hop EP in collaboration with Creole Haitian rap stars such as Seca Konsa and among others.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He is cousins with <a href="/wiki/DJ_Whoo_Kid" title="DJ Whoo Kid">DJ Whoo Kid</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
