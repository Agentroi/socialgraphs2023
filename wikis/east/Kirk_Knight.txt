<p><b>Kirlan Labarrie</b> (born November 16, 1995), better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Kirk Knight</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">record producer</a>, rapper and member of the <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop</a> collective <a href="/wiki/Pro_Era" title="Pro Era">Pro Era</a>. His debut project, <i><a href="/wiki/Late_Knight_Special" title="Late Knight Special">Late Knight Special</a></i>, was released on October 30, 2015. On July 21, 2017, he released a collaborative project with fellow Pro Era member Nyck Caution, <i>Nyck @ Knight</i>, with its first single "Off the Wall" released on June 8, 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Kirk Knight is both a producer as well as a rapper. Kirk has produced top songs such as Flem by A$AP Ferg and Big Dusty by Joey Bada$$.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> His debut studio album, Late Night Special, peaked on the Billboard charts on November 21, 2015 at number 33. Lasting one week on the charts.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Recently, Kirk Knight as well as Joey Bada$$ of the Pro Era collective rap group have made hints at a new solo album coming out in 2018 for Kirk Knight.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Kirk Knight also went on tour in 2018 with rap collective Flatbush Zombies along with fellow Pro Era member Nyck Caution.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
