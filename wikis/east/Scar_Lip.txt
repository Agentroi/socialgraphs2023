<p><b>Sierra Lewis</b>, known professionally as <b>Scar Lip</b>, is an American rapper. Born December 12th, 2000.  Her 2022 single "Glizzy Gobbler" went viral on <a href="/wiki/TikTok" title="TikTok">TikTok</a> and she was signed to <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records" title="Epic Records">Epic Records</a> the following year after releasing her breakout single "This is New York".
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scar_Lip&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sierra Lewis<sup id="cite_ref-hnhhwho_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hnhhwho-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was born in <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn" title="Brooklyn">Brooklyn</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> and raised in <a href="/wiki/The_Bronx" title="The Bronx">The Bronx</a>, <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-h97_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-h97-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> She began writing poetry from a young age.<sup id="cite_ref-animconvo_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-animconvo-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> At age 12, her mother died after getting into a <a href="/wiki/Hit_and_run" title="Hit and run">hit and run</a> car accident. At age 16, she got a scar on her lip after her brother punched her in the mouth while she tried to get into her house; the scar later became the basis for her stage name. According to her, she was abused by her aunt and uncle while living with them.<sup id="cite_ref-hnhhwho_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hnhhwho-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> She was eventually kicked out of her aunt's house and put into foster care, which led to her often moving between foster homes and detention centers in places such as Pennsylvania and <a href="/wiki/Coney_Island" title="Coney Island">Coney Island</a>. She was first inspired to pursue music by her admiration for rapper <a href="/wiki/DMX" title="DMX">DMX</a> and posted her first rap video online in 2018, which went viral online the following year.<sup id="cite_ref-animconvo_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-animconvo-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Her first three songs, "Therapy", "Suicide Awareness", and "Foster Care", were recorded from 2019 to 2020.<sup id="cite_ref-xxlbreak_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-xxlbreak-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Scar Lip first gained popularity with her 2022 single "Glizzy Gobbler", which went viral on <a href="/wiki/TikTok" title="TikTok">TikTok</a> as a meme.<sup id="cite_ref-xxlbreak_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-xxlbreak-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Her single "This is New York" was produced by NY Bangers and was released in April 2023, the same month that she was featured on the <a href="/wiki/Swizz_Beatz" title="Swizz Beatz">Swizz Beatz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jadakiss" title="Jadakiss">Jadakiss</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Benny_the_Butcher" title="Benny the Butcher">Benny the Butcher</a> song "Take 'Em Out" from Swizz Beatz's extended play (EP) <i>Hip Hop 50: Vol. 2</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hhdxcb_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hhdxcb-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> "This is New York" became popular on <a href="/wiki/TikTok" title="TikTok">TikTok</a> and soon became her breakout hit, while a remix featuring <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a> titled "This is Cali" was released in May 2023.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ahh_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ahh-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> She was signed to <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records" title="Epic Records">Epic Records</a> in June 2023.<sup id="cite_ref-epicrec_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-epicrec-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Scar Lip's upcoming debut EP, <i>Scars &amp; Stripes</i>, will be produced entirely by Swizz Beatz.<sup id="cite_ref-xxlbreak_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-xxlbreak-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_style">Musical style</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Scar_Lip&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Musical style">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Scar Lip's music is primarily <a href="/wiki/East_Coast_hip_hop" title="East Coast hip hop">New York hip hop</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gangsta_rap" title="Gangsta rap">gangsta rap</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ahh_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ahh-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> She has listed DMX, the hip hop group <a href="/wiki/Onyx_(hip_hop_group)" title="Onyx (hip hop group)">Onyx</a>, and rapper <a href="/wiki/Cardi_B" title="Cardi B">Cardi B</a> as influences on her music and described her sound as "hardcore aggression".<sup id="cite_ref-hnhhwho_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hnhhwho-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-animconvo_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-animconvo-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-epicrec_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-epicrec-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
