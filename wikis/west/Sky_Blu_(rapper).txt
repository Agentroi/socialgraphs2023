<p><b>Skyler Austen Gordy</b> (born August 23, 1986),<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Sky Blu</b> (stylized as <b>8ky 6lu</b>),<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, DJ and dancer best known as one half of the musical duo <a href="/wiki/LMFAO" title="LMFAO">LMFAO</a>, with the hit song "<a href="/wiki/Party_Rock_Anthem" title="Party Rock Anthem">Party Rock Anthem</a>". He formed the duo with his paternal half-uncle, <a href="/wiki/Redfoo" title="Redfoo">Redfoo</a>, and released two albums together,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> 2009's <i><a href="/wiki/Party_Rock" title="Party Rock">Party Rock</a></i> and 2011's <i><a href="/wiki/Sorry_for_Party_Rocking" title="Sorry for Party Rocking">Sorry for Party Rocking</a></i>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sky_Blu_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2006, Gordy, under the stage name <b>Sky Blu</b> teamed up with his uncle <a href="/wiki/Redfoo" title="Redfoo">Redfoo</a> to form <a href="/wiki/LMFAO" title="LMFAO">LMFAO</a>. The band started in Miami, Florida and released rap songs over electronic beats.<sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> They are known for their hit songs "<a href="/wiki/Party_Rock_Anthem" title="Party Rock Anthem">Party Rock Anthem</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Sexy_and_I_Know_It" title="Sexy and I Know It">Sexy and I Know It</a>," which were released in 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> "Party Rock Anthem" became the most successful song of their career, peaking at number one in over ten countries, including France, the UK, the U.S., Canada, New Zealand, Germany, Ireland, and Australia. Redfoo announced that LMFAO would go on hiatus in 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-ds_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ds-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Earlier that year, LMFAO performed alongside <a href="/wiki/Madonna" title="Madonna">Madonna</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Super_Bowl_XLVI" title="Super Bowl XLVI">Super Bowl XLVI</a> halftime.<sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>  Gordy later claimed in a Facebook post in 2016 that Redfoo had tried to claim all of the band's royalties for himself and that the latter had announced LMFAO's hiatus a year after not speaking with Gordy, who had suffered serious back injury.<sup id="cite_ref-ds_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ds-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>In October 2010, Gordy and his girlfriend Chelsea Korka created BIG BAD out of their Hollywood home which is an entertainment company consisting of musicians, artists, producers, DJ's, inventors, creators, and had successful tours in America, Dominican Republic, South America, Asia, and parts of Europe.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Gordy also created Big Bad University ("the first university for dreamers"), a collective of artists, which was mainly composed of rapper <a href="/wiki/Shwayze" title="Shwayze">Shwayze</a>, Korka, and producer/singer/songwriter Mark Rosas. 
</p><p>In 2013, Sky released his two first singles "Pop Bottles" and "Salud".<sup id="cite_ref-ds_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ds-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> That year he also released his 9-track first album <i>Rebel Music</i>, and a second album <i>We Evolve Everyday</i> as free downloads.<sup id="cite_ref-ds_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ds-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In 2014, Gordy introduced to the world his alter ego <b>The Party President</b> and a new stylization of his name <b>8ky 6lu</b>, follwowed by the release his single "Go On Girl". Gordy explained that the numbers 86 represented his birth year.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>  In December that year, Gordy was invited to the <a href="/wiki/Miss_World" title="Miss World">Miss World</a> ceremony to perform his single "We Love Girls" with KG Superstar. Sky released an album, <i>Chaos to Consciousness</i> under his new artist name <b>8ky 6ordy</b> (pronounced Sky Gordy) in 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-ds_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ds-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sky_Blu_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gordy is the grandson of <a href="/wiki/Motown" title="Motown">Motown</a> founder <a href="/wiki/Berry_Gordy" title="Berry Gordy">Berry Gordy</a>. He is the son of Berry Gordy IV and Valerie Robeson. He is the brother of DJ and singer Mahogany "Lox" Cheyenne Gordy. Other relatives include singer-songwriters <a href="/wiki/Rockwell_(musician)" title="Rockwell (musician)">Rockwell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rhonda_Ross_Kendrick" title="Rhonda Ross Kendrick">Rhonda Ross Kendrick</a>, who are his uncle and aunt via his grandfather Berry Gordy, respectively. According to Gordy, he attended 12 different schools growing up and ultimately dropped out of high school without taking the GED.<sup id="cite_ref-mb_4-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mb-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In January 2009, <a href="/wiki/Interscope_Records" title="Interscope Records">Interscope Records</a> arranged a studio session for <a href="/wiki/Paradiso_Girls" title="Paradiso Girls">Paradiso Girls</a> with LMFAO where Chelsea Korka met Sky Blu. A friendship which then bloomed into a long term relationship shortly after. Chelsea was featured in many of LMFAO's music videos as Sky Blu's Love Interest <sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> and they were very public about their affection for one another.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
