<p><b>The Regime</b> (stylized as <b>The ReGime</b>) is an American <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop</a> <a href="/wiki/Collective" title="Collective">collective</a> created by <a href="/wiki/Yukmouth" title="Yukmouth">Yukmouth</a> in 1997, and originally included fellow rappers <a href="/wiki/Tech_N9ne" title="Tech N9ne">Tech N9ne</a>, Phats Bossi, Madmax, <a href="/wiki/Poppa_LQ" class="mw-redirect" title="Poppa LQ">Poppa LQ</a>, Dizzle Don and Govnormatic.<sup id="cite_ref-SAL-Bio_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SAL-Bio-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Not long after the original formation, Gonzoe of rap group <a href="/wiki/Kausion" title="Kausion">Kausion</a> and Lil Ke also joined.<sup id="cite_ref-SAL-Bio_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SAL-Bio-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The group is signed to Yukmouth's <a href="/wiki/Smoke-A-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Smoke-A-Lot Records">Smoke-A-Lot Records</a>, and share that label's dragon logo.
</p><p>The Regime is unique when it comes to rap groups in that the members are from different states and cities around the U.S.. Besides guest appearances on numerous Yukmouth related projects, the group has released three mixtapes in the <i>All Out War</i> series and have plans for their debut album to be titled <i>Regime Dragon Gang</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-dubcnn_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-dubcnn-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-baycentrik_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-baycentrik-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
