<p><b>Deshawn Carter</b> (born 1982), better known by his stage name <b>Parlay Starr</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> from <a href="/wiki/South_Los_Angeles,_California" class="mw-redirect" title="South Los Angeles, California">South Los Angeles, California</a>, United States. 
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parlay_Starr&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born <b>Deshawn Carter</b> in 1982, he grew up in the Pueblos Housing Projects of south-central Los Angeles.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He attended <a href="/wiki/Crenshaw_High_School" title="Crenshaw High School">Crenshaw High School</a>, where he played basketball, and thus met some of the actors and rap artists who came to watch the school games.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> While at school he considered becoming a rap artist, though he initially chose basketball as a career.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He attended Blaine College in Augusta, Georgia, on a basketball scholarship, but left after two years to care for his grandmother.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After his grandmother's death he returned to college, but found he was seven degree credits short.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_career">Musical career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parlay_Starr&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Musical career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 1999, Carter met and began to perform with the group <a href="/wiki/2nd_II_None" title="2nd II None">2nd II None</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Soon thereafter he was introduced to <a href="/wiki/DJ_Quik" title="DJ Quik">DJ Quik</a>, and began to tour nationally.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In 2005, Carter connected with <a href="/wiki/Tha_Realest" title="Tha Realest">Tha Realest</a>, and they went on to perform together for several years.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Since then Carter has collaborated with <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a> on "All Out of Love",<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="Each facts in a biography of a living person needs a citation to verify that fact. Uncited facts may be removed. (January 2022)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> and has met several other artists, including <a href="/wiki/Too_Short" title="Too Short">Too Short</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jim_Jones" title="Jim Jones">Jim Jones</a>, <a href="/wiki/Terrence_Howard" title="Terrence Howard">Terrence Howard</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Outlawz" title="Outlawz">Outlawz</a>, who encouraged him to break into his own solo career.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2009, Carter created Most Hated Mob Entertainment, and began to release his own mixtapes.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In 2010, he released the album <i>Welcome to my World</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> featuring guest appearances by <a href="/wiki/Tha_Realest" title="Tha Realest">Tha Realest</a>, <a href="/wiki/Yukmouth" title="Yukmouth">Yukmouth</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Outlawz" title="Outlawz">Outlawz</a>, and the track "Like You" with <a href="/wiki/Chris_Brown_(American_singer)" class="mw-redirect" title="Chris Brown (American singer)">Chris Brown</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Tyga" title="Tyga">Tyga</a>, also featured on Chris Brown's "Takers" Mixtape.
</p><p>In 2012, Carter released a mixtape entitled <i>Starrface</i>, hosted By <a href="/wiki/Dj_Whoo_Kid" class="mw-redirect" title="Dj Whoo Kid">Dj Whoo Kid</a> from Gunit Records.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="Each facts in a biography of a living person needs a citation to verify that fact. Uncited facts may be removed. (January 2022)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Parlay_Starr&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Carter relocated to <a href="/wiki/Bozeman,_MT" class="mw-redirect" title="Bozeman, MT">Bozeman</a>, Montana in 2011 with his family. As his partner originates from the <a href="/wiki/Gallatin_County,_Montana" title="Gallatin County, Montana">Gallatin Valley</a> of southwest Montana, they decided to relocate back there after their son was born, as they did not want him growing up on the city streets of South Central Los Angeles.<sup id="cite_ref-Hill_1-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hill-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Carter is a devout <a href="/wiki/Christians" title="Christians">Christian</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2023)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
