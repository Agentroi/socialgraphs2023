<p><b>Davionte Ganter</b>, known professionally as <b>GaTa</b>, is an American rapper and actor known for his role in the <a href="/wiki/FXX" title="FXX">FXX</a> TV series <a href="/wiki/Dave_(TV_series)" title="Dave (TV series)"><i>Dave</i></a>, as well as for being the <a href="/wiki/Hype_man" title="Hype man">hype man</a> for rapper <a href="/wiki/Lil_Dicky" title="Lil Dicky">Lil Dicky</a> throughout his career as well as on the show.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GaTa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>GaTa grew up in <a href="/wiki/South_Los_Angeles" title="South Los Angeles">South Los Angeles</a>, where he was adopted out of foster care by an aunt whom he considers his mother. He never knew his biological mother or father.<sup id="cite_ref-Wong_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wong-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Inspired by the rapper <a href="/wiki/Da_Brat" title="Da Brat">Da Brat</a>, he began rapping while a student at Audubon Middle School.<sup id="cite_ref-Wood_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GaTa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Music">Music</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GaTa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Music">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As a <a href="/wiki/Hype_man" title="Hype man">hype man</a>, GaTa traveled the world performing with rappers including <a href="/wiki/Lil_Wayne" title="Lil Wayne">Lil Wayne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gym_Class_Heroes" title="Gym Class Heroes">Gym Class Heroes</a>. <a href="/wiki/Tyga" title="Tyga">Tyga</a>, GaTa, and his childhood friend <a href="/wiki/Schoolboy_Q" title="Schoolboy Q">Schoolboy Q</a> formed the music label G.E.D., which GaTa says stands for "grinding every day, getting every dollar, getting educated daily."<sup id="cite_ref-Wood_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wood-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Around 2013, GaTa began working with <a href="/wiki/David_Burd" class="mw-redirect" title="David Burd">David Burd</a>, known professionally as <a href="/wiki/Lil_Dicky" title="Lil Dicky">Lil Dicky</a>, who would become his close friend and with whom he would later star on the sitcom <i>Dave</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Jones_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jones-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Wong_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wong-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> After gaining popularity as an actor, he continued to build his own burgeoning solo rap career, most recently dropping the single "Check Up" in July 2021.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Television_and_film">Television and film</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GaTa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Television and film">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>GaTa began his acting career in the <a href="/wiki/FXX" title="FXX">FXX</a> TV series <a href="/wiki/Dave_(TV_series)" title="Dave (TV series)"><i>Dave</i></a>, in which he plays an authentic but "heightened" version of himself.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Having never acted beyond music videos or taken any acting classes, he had to audition to play the role of himself on the show.<sup id="cite_ref-Jones_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jones-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-KTLA_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KTLA-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> His performance has been well received, leading some critics to say he deserved an <a href="/wiki/Emmy_Awards" title="Emmy Awards">Emmy</a> nomination.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Menta_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Menta-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2022, GaTa appeared in the <a href="/wiki/Stoner_film" title="Stoner film">stoner film</a> <i><a href="/wiki/Good_Mourning_(film)" title="Good Mourning (film)">Good Mourning</a></i> with <a href="/wiki/Machine_Gun_Kelly_(musician)" title="Machine Gun Kelly (musician)">Machine Gun Kelly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mod_Sun" title="Mod Sun">Mod Sun</a>. GaTa also appeared on season 1, episode 10 of Apple TV’s <i><a href="/wiki/Loot_(TV_series)" title="Loot (TV series)">Loot</a></i> as himself.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=GaTa&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>GaTa has <a href="/wiki/Bipolar_disorder" title="Bipolar disorder">bipolar disorder</a>, a condition that was also given to his character on <i>Dave</i>. He takes medication for the disorder.<sup id="cite_ref-Menta_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Menta-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He was initially reluctant to talk about bipolar disorder on the show, but his family and David Burd convinced him that doing so could help others.<sup id="cite_ref-Wong_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wong-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
