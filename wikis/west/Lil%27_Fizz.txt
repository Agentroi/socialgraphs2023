<p><b>Dreux Pierre Frédéric</b> (born November 26, 1985) better known by his stage name <b>Lil' Fizz</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a>, singer, songwriter and actor best known for being the youngest member and rapper of the <a href="/wiki/Rhythm_and_Blues" class="mw-redirect" title="Rhythm and Blues">R&amp;B</a> group <a href="/wiki/B2K" title="B2K">B2K</a>. Formerly, he starred on the television show <i><a href="/wiki/Love_%26_Hip_Hop:_Hollywood" title="Love &amp; Hip Hop: Hollywood">Love &amp; Hip Hop: Hollywood</a></i>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lil%27_Fizz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Frédéric was born in <a href="/wiki/New_Orleans,_Louisiana" class="mw-redirect" title="New Orleans, Louisiana">New Orleans, Louisiana</a> and raised in Los Angeles, California. He is of <a href="/wiki/Louisiana_Creole_people" title="Louisiana Creole people">Louisiana Creole</a> heritage.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lil%27_Fizz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Known as the rapper of the group, Lil' Fizz was a member of <a href="/wiki/B2K" title="B2K">B2K</a> from 2000 to 2004. After the group broke up, he began his independent rap career.
His debut EP, <i>Payday</i>, was released on July 17, 2007, on <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a> as a digital download release.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He reconnected with former fellow B2K member J-Boog and long time friend and business manager Damuer H. Leffridge to launch a new record label, Popular Entertainment. Under Popular Ent., they released a 5-song EP titled <i>Night Life</i>, available for digital download on iTunes in September 2009.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lil%27_Fizz&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He has a son, Kameron, born in 2010, with Moniece Slaughter.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He and Slaughter have both appeared on <i><a href="/wiki/Love_%26_Hip_Hop:_Hollywood" title="Love &amp; Hip Hop: Hollywood">Love &amp; Hip Hop: Hollywood</a></i> since its inception in 2014.
</p><p>In <a href="/wiki/Love_%26_Hip_Hop:_Hollywood_(season_6)" title="Love &amp; Hip Hop: Hollywood (season 6)">season 6</a> (2019), Fizz revealed that he was in a romantic relationship with Apryl Jones, who was a fellow Love &amp; Hip Hop cast member and the ex-girlfriend and babies' mother of fellow B2K member <a href="/wiki/Omarion" title="Omarion">Omarion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> As of 2020, he and Apryl have ended their relationship.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Lingering issues between the two were addressed in <i><a href="/wiki/VH1_Family_Reunion:_Love_%26_Hip_Hop_Edition" class="mw-redirect" title="VH1 Family Reunion: Love &amp; Hip Hop Edition">VH1 Family Reunion: Love &amp; Hip Hop Edition</a></i>, which was filmed in 2020 during the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a> and aired in 2021.
</p><p>He also appeared in <i><a href="/wiki/Marriage_Boot_Camp" title="Marriage Boot Camp">Marriage Boot Camp: Hip-Hop Edition</a></i> in 2019 with his girlfriend at that time, Tiffany Campbell.
</p>
