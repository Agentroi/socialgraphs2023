					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" lang="en" dir="ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">American rapper</div>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1126788409">.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul{line-height:inherit;list-style:none;margin:0;padding:0}.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol li,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul li{margin-bottom:0}</style><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1066479718">.mw-parser-output .infobox-subbox{padding:0;border:none;margin:-3px;width:auto;min-width:100%;font-size:100%;clear:none;float:none;background-color:transparent}.mw-parser-output .infobox-3cols-child{margin:auto}.mw-parser-output .infobox .navbar{font-size:100%}body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-header,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-subheader,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-above,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-title,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-image,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-full-data,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-below{text-align:center}</style><table class="infobox vcard plainlist"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-above" style="background-color: #b0c4de; font-size: 125%;"><div class="">Black Pegasus</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Birth name</span></th><td class="infobox-data nickname">Robert Houston II</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Also known as</span></th><td class="infobox-data nickname">Black-P<br />The Black Mexican<br />Yo Mamma's Pimp</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Born</th><td class="infobox-data"><span style="display:none"> (<span class="bday">1980-03-24</span>) </span>March 24, 1980<span class="noprint ForceAgeToShow"> (age&#160;43)</span><br />Germany</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Origin</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Colorado_Springs,_Colorado" title="Colorado Springs, Colorado">Colorado Springs, Colorado</a>, U.S.</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Genres</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">Hip hop</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Occupation(s)</th><td class="infobox-data role"><a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">Rapper</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Years active</span></th><td class="infobox-data">2001-present</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Labels</th><td class="infobox-data">Brass Knuckle Entertainment</td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-header" style="background-color: #b0c4de"></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Website</th><td class="infobox-data"><a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://Iamblackp.com/">[1]</a></td></tr></tbody></table><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">Musical artist</div>
<p><b>Robert Houston II</b>, also known as <b>Black Pegasus</b> or simply <b>Black-P</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a>/South Western hip hop artist from <a href="/wiki/Colorado_Springs,_Colorado" title="Colorado Springs, Colorado">Colorado Springs</a>. He is the current president and CEO of Brass Knuckle Entertainment.<sup id="cite_ref-Stanley_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Stanley-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Black_Pegasus&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Robert Houston II was born on March 24, 1980<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> where his father was stationed with the <a href="/wiki/US_Air_Force" class="mw-redirect" title="US Air Force">US Air Force</a>, and lived in Arizona, Florida, and New Mexico before his family settled on the Southeast side of <a href="/wiki/Colorado_Springs,_Colorado" title="Colorado Springs, Colorado">Colorado Springs</a> when he was age ten.<sup id="cite_ref-Reed_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Reed-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Forman_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forman-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Short_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Short-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He is from the Woodside Townhomes section in Colorado Springs. He is of mixed African-American and Mexican descent, hence the title of his fourth album, <i>The Black Mexican</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Forman_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forman-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He open enrolled for high school and attended Widefield and Mitchell High School, and began rapping when he was sixteen, soon performing and working in his early days under the name Yo Mamma's Pimp.<sup id="cite_ref-Forman_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forman-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He worked with the group Fusion of Syllables (F.O.S.) for two years before going solo in 2001.<sup id="cite_ref-Forman_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forman-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Navarro_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Navarro-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Black-P has released five studio albums (<i>Black Pegasus</i>, <i>Knuckle Up</i>, <i>F**K YO RADIO</i>, <i>The Black Mexican</i>, and <i>Black By Popular Demand</i>), two mixtapes (<i>Black P The Mixtape</i>, and <i>Shitting On the Industry Vol.1</i>), and one collaboration CD (<i>Banana Spitz</i>) with Liquid Assassin of the hip-hop group Grave Plott. His new album <i>Brass Knuckle King</i> was released in 2011, and featured guest appearances by <a href="/wiki/Immortal_Technique" title="Immortal Technique">Immortal Technique</a>, Liquid Assassin, and <a href="/wiki/Kutt_Calhoun" title="Kutt Calhoun">Kutt Calhoun</a>. Black P is also currently working on a collaboration album entitled "The Council", which will consist of Black P, King Tef, Hypnautic, and Johnny Rocketz. Black P's seventh studio album, "Yo Mamma's Pimp", is slated for a 2012 release as well.
</p><p>Black-P has taken part in several battle-rapping contests,<sup id="cite_ref-Jefferson_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Jefferson-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> winning the Rock the Mic Battle in 2003 and taking second place in Scribble Jam in Cincinnati.<sup id="cite_ref-Reed_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Reed-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Forman_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forman-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He has featured on the <a href="/wiki/MTV" title="MTV">MTV</a> shows <i><a href="/wiki/Road_Rules" title="Road Rules">Road Rules</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Real_World_(TV_series)" title="The Real World (TV series)">The Real World</a></i> and in the <i>NBA Jam 2</i> video game.<sup id="cite_ref-Navarro_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Navarro-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Fan_Base">Fan Base</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Black_Pegasus&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Fan Base">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Black Pegasus also has a very large following in <a href="/wiki/Denver,_Colorado" class="mw-redirect" title="Denver, Colorado">Denver</a>. He has gained much notoriety throughout the <a href="/wiki/Midwestern_United_States" title="Midwestern United States">Midwest</a> and <a href="/wiki/Southwestern_United_States" title="Southwestern United States">Southwestern United States</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
