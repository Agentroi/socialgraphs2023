<p><b>Christen Kelley</b> (November 7, 1978 – October 2, 2017), better known by his stage name <b>Slip Capone</b>,
was an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> and member of <a href="/wiki/Tha_Dogg_Pound" title="Tha Dogg Pound">DPGC</a> from <a href="/wiki/Hawthorne,_California" title="Hawthorne, California">Hawthorne, California</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Slip_Capone&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Slip Capone was born on November 7, 1978. He grew up on 118th street in Hawthorne California.
He is half British and half African American and is commonly referred to<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Words_to_watch#Unsupported_attributions" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Words to watch"><span title="The material near this tag may use weasel words or too-vague attribution. (October 2020)">by whom?</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> as “The Mayor of Hawthorne".
Christen Kelley was signed to <a href="/wiki/Death_Row_Records" title="Death Row Records">Death Row Records</a> in the mid 90's when he was 14 years old.
He then went on to appear on <a href="/wiki/Kurupt" title="Kurupt">Kurupt</a>’s solo debut album <i><a href="/wiki/Kuruption!" title="Kuruption!">Kuruption!</a></i> on a song called "Survive Another Day" by Kurupt featuring <a href="/wiki/Gonzoe" class="mw-redirect" title="Gonzoe">Gonzo</a> and himself.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_career">Early career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Slip_Capone&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During his teenage years, Slip Capone met rapper <a href="/wiki/Snoop_Dogg" title="Snoop Dogg">Snoop Dogg</a> through Kurupt of the <a href="/wiki/Dogg_Pound" class="mw-redirect" title="Dogg Pound">Dogg Pound</a>. Proving himself with his rapping skills, his first appearance was on Snoop Dogg’s multiplatinum <i><a href="/wiki/Murder_Was_the_Case" title="Murder Was the Case">Murder Was the Case</a></i> soundtrack with a song called "The Eulogy" by Slip Capone &amp; <a href="/wiki/CPO_Boss_Hogg" title="CPO Boss Hogg">CPO Boss Hogg</a>.
</p><p>In the United States, on the chart dated November 5, 1994, <i>Murder Was the Case</i> debuted on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200"><i>Billboard</i> 200</a> at number one, with first week sales of 329,000 units.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> The album opened at the top spot of the <a href="/wiki/Top_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Albums" title="Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums">R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums</a> chart.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>The following week it stayed on top with 197,000 copies sold and was certified Gold.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> The album is certified 2× platinum with 2,030,000 copies sold.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
