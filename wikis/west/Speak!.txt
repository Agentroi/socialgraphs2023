<p><b>Anthony David Negrete</b> (born February 24, 1987), better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage names</a> <b>Speak!</b> and <b>Speakz</b>, is an American rapper and songwriter from <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" class="mw-redirect" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>. He is known for having co-written the 2011 song "<a href="/wiki/Gucci_Gucci" title="Gucci Gucci">Gucci Gucci</a>" performed by <a href="/wiki/Kreayshawn" title="Kreayshawn">Kreayshawn</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and collaborating with various Odd Future members such as <a href="/wiki/Mike_G" class="mw-redirect" title="Mike G">Mike G</a> and <a href="/wiki/Syd_(singer)" title="Syd (singer)">Syd tha Kyd</a>, who recorded his 2011 album <i>Inside Out Boy</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He is based in Mexico City.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources.
