<p><b>Jerold Dwight Ellis III</b> (born October 18, 1974),<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> better known by his stage name <b>Yukmouth</b>, is an American rapper. He is the founder of <a href="/wiki/The_Regime_(group)" title="The Regime (group)">The Regime</a>, <a href="/wiki/Smoke-A-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Smoke-A-Lot Records">Smoke-A-Lot Records</a> and <a href="/wiki/Godzilla_Entertainment" class="mw-redirect" title="Godzilla Entertainment">Godzilla Entertainment</a>. He is a member of <a href="/wiki/Thug_Lordz" class="mw-redirect" title="Thug Lordz">Thug Lordz</a> with <a href="/wiki/C-Bo" title="C-Bo">C-Bo</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yukmouth&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Yukmouth is a member of the rap duo <a href="/wiki/Luniz" title="Luniz">Luniz</a>, along with <a href="/wiki/Numskull" class="mw-redirect" title="Numskull">Numskull</a>. Luniz recorded the <a href="/wiki/Cannabis_(drug)" title="Cannabis (drug)">weed</a> anthem, "<a href="/wiki/I_Got_5_on_It" title="I Got 5 on It">I Got 5 on It</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-:1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Yukmouth signed a solo record deal at <a href="/wiki/James_Smith_(record_executive)" class="mw-redirect" title="James Smith (record executive)">J-Prince's</a> <a href="/wiki/Rap-a-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Rap-a-Lot Records">Rap-a-Lot Records</a>, releasing four albums. His double album debut on the label, entitled <i><a href="/wiki/Thugged_Out:_The_Albulation" title="Thugged Out: The Albulation">Thugged Out: The Albulation</a></i>, went gold.<sup id="cite_ref-RIAA-Yuk_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RIAA-Yuk-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Under his own label, <a href="/wiki/Smoke-a-Lot_Records" title="Smoke-a-Lot Records">Smoke-a-Lot Records</a>, he has released <a href="/wiki/DVD" title="DVD">DVDs</a> and <a href="/wiki/Compilation_album" title="Compilation album">compilation albums</a>, such as the <i><a href="/wiki/United_Ghettos_of_America" title="United Ghettos of America">United Ghettos of America</a></i> series.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Smoke-A-Lot">Smoke-A-Lot</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yukmouth&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Smoke-A-Lot">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1033289096">.mw-parser-output .hatnote{font-style:italic}.mw-parser-output div.hatnote{padding-left:1.6em;margin-bottom:0.5em}.mw-parser-output .hatnote i{font-style:normal}.mw-parser-output .hatnote+link+.hatnote{margin-top:-0.5em}</style><div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Smoke-a-Lot_Records" title="Smoke-a-Lot Records">Smoke-a-Lot Records</a></div>
<p>Yukmouth is CEO of <a href="/wiki/Smoke-a-Lot_Records" title="Smoke-a-Lot Records">Smoke-A-Lot</a>, which is distributed by <a href="/wiki/Rap-a-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Rap-a-Lot Records">Rap-a-Lot Records</a>. The label is home to established artists such as <a href="/wiki/Luniz" title="Luniz">Luniz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dru_Down" title="Dru Down">Dru Down</a>, Thug Lordz (Yukmouth, <a href="/wiki/C-Bo" title="C-Bo">C-Bo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Spice_1" title="Spice 1">Spice 1</a>), <a href="/wiki/The_Regime_(group)" title="The Regime (group)">The Regime</a>, and Yukmouth himself.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Godzilla_Entertainment">Godzilla Entertainment</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yukmouth&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Godzilla Entertainment">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<p><b>Godzilla Entertainment</b> was an American <a href="/wiki/Record_label" title="Record label">record label</a> founded and owned by rapper Yukmouth of the popular 1990s rap group <a href="/wiki/The_Luniz" class="mw-redirect" title="The Luniz">The Luniz</a>, and operated by both Yukmouth and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Kat_Gaynor&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Kat Gaynor (page does not exist)">Kat Gaynor</a> (who also acts as Yukmouth and <a href="/wiki/The_Regime_(group)" title="The Regime (group)">The Regime</a>'s manager).  The name comes from Yukmouth's popular 2003 album, <a href="/wiki/Godzilla_(Yukmouth_album)" title="Godzilla (Yukmouth album)">Godzilla</a>. It was initially created to serve as an independent record distribution company for official mixtapes released on <a href="/wiki/Smoke-A-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Smoke-A-Lot Records">Smoke-A-Lot Records</a>.  The first few releases through Godzilla was the heavily popular <i>All Out War</i> mixtape series which were done as a joint venture with the Cali Untouchable DJs and Rapbay.com.  The initial logo featured an edited version of the <a href="/wiki/Aston_Martin" title="Aston Martin">Aston Martin</a> logo which was later replaced by a more independent logo which features the <a href="/wiki/Smoke-A-Lot_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Smoke-A-Lot Records">Smoke-A-Lot Records</a> <a href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon">dragon</a> logo encircled by the words "<i>Godzilla Ent.</i>".  In May 2007 Yukmouth closed down the label and merged all of its duties with the parent Smoke-A-Lot Records after pressure from <a href="/wiki/Toho_Co._Ltd." class="mw-redirect" title="Toho Co. Ltd.">Toho Co. Ltd.</a>, the owners of the name <i>Godzilla</i>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Closure">Closure</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Yukmouth&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Closure">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Upon receiving pressure from <a href="/wiki/Toho_Company_Ltd." class="mw-redirect" title="Toho Company Ltd.">the owners</a> of the copyright to the name Godzilla, Yukmouth closed down the Godzilla subset label and now releases projects solely on the Smoke-A-Lot Records imprint. All media pertaining to the name Godzilla, including logos, t-shirts and other media were removed from the official Smoke-A-Lot website shortly after.
</p>
