<p><b>Flash Bang Grenada</b> is an American hip hop duo based in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It consists of <a href="/wiki/Busdriver" title="Busdriver">Busdriver</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nocando" title="Nocando">Nocando</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Flash_Bang_Grenada&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Flash Bang Grenada released the debut album, <i><a href="/wiki/10_Haters" title="10 Haters">10 Haters</a></i>, on <a href="/wiki/Hellfyre_Club" title="Hellfyre Club">Hellfyre Club</a> in 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It featured vocal contributions from <a href="/wiki/Open_Mike_Eagle" title="Open Mike Eagle">Open Mike Eagle</a> and <a href="/wiki/Del_the_Funky_Homosapien" title="Del the Funky Homosapien">Del the Funky Homosapien</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> It was chosen by <i>Alarm</i> as one of the 50 Unheralded Albums from 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
