<p><b>Tyruss Gerald Himes</b> (November 22, 1968 –  December 5, 2016), better known by his stage names <b>Big Syke</b> and <b>Mussolini</b>, was an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> best known for his work with the American hip-hop groups <a href="/wiki/Thug_Life" title="Thug Life">Thug Life</a> and <a href="/wiki/Outlawz" title="Outlawz">Outlawz</a>. His stage name "Big Syke" is a revision of his childhood nickname "Little Psycho".<sup id="cite_ref-allmusicbio_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-allmusicbio-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He died at his home in <a href="/wiki/Hawthorne,_California" title="Hawthorne, California">Hawthorne, California</a> on December 5, 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-tmz_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tmz-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Big_Syke&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Syke was born and raised in <a href="/wiki/Inglewood,_California" title="Inglewood, California">Inglewood, California</a>. He was a member of the <a href="/wiki/Crips" title="Crips">Imperial Village Crips</a> in <a href="/wiki/Inglewood,_California" title="Inglewood, California">Inglewood, California</a>. In 1990, Big Syke and fellow rappers Domino and Mental Illness started a hip-hop group named Evil Mind Gangstas.<sup id="cite_ref-allmusicbio_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-allmusicbio-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In 1992 he met rapper <a href="/wiki/Tupac_Shakur" title="Tupac Shakur">Tupac "2Pac" Shakur</a><sup id="cite_ref-allmusicbio_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-allmusicbio-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and joined 2Pac's rap group <a href="/wiki/Thug_Life" title="Thug Life">Thug Life</a>. In 1995 he joined 2Pac's second rap group <a href="/wiki/Outlawz" title="Outlawz">The Outlaw Immortalz</a> as Mussolini and recorded songs for 2Pac's 1996 album <i><a href="/wiki/All_Eyez_on_Me" title="All Eyez on Me">All Eyez on Me</a></i>, including "Picture Me Rollin'", "When We Ride", "All Eyez on Me", and "Check Out Time".<sup id="cite_ref-tmz_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tmz-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Death">Death</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Big_Syke&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Death">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Big Syke died at his home in <a href="/wiki/Hawthorne,_California" title="Hawthorne, California">Hawthorne, California</a> on December 5, 2016, at the age of 48 from natural causes.<sup id="cite_ref-tmz_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tmz-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
