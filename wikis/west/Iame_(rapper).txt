					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" lang="en" dir="ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">American rapper</div>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1126788409">.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul{line-height:inherit;list-style:none;margin:0;padding:0}.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol li,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul li{margin-bottom:0}</style><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1066479718">.mw-parser-output .infobox-subbox{padding:0;border:none;margin:-3px;width:auto;min-width:100%;font-size:100%;clear:none;float:none;background-color:transparent}.mw-parser-output .infobox-3cols-child{margin:auto}.mw-parser-output .infobox .navbar{font-size:100%}body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-header,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-subheader,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-above,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-title,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-image,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-full-data,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-below{text-align:center}</style><table class="infobox vcard plainlist"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-above" style="background-color: #b0c4de; font-size: 125%;"><div class="">Iame</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Birth name</span></th><td class="infobox-data nickname">Ryan McMahon</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Also known as</span></th><td class="infobox-data nickname">Wool See</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Origin</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Portland,_Oregon" title="Portland, Oregon">Portland, Oregon</a>, United States</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Genres</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">Hip hop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alternative_hip_hop" title="Alternative hip hop">alternative hip hop</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Northwest_hip_hop" class="mw-redirect" title="Pacific Northwest hip hop">Pacific Northwest hip hop</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Occupation(s)</th><td class="infobox-data role"><a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">Rapper</a>, <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_production" title="Hip hop production">producer</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Years active</span></th><td class="infobox-data">2003-present</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Labels</th><td class="infobox-data">Heaven Noise, Taxidermy Records</td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-header" style="background-color: #b0c4de"></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Website</th><td class="infobox-data"><span class="url"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://heavennoise.com">heavennoise<wbr />.com</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">Musical artist</div>
<p><b>Ryan McMahon</b>, better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Iame</b> (pronounced <span class="rt-commentedText nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt" lang="en-fonipa"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="/aɪ/: &#39;i&#39; in &#39;tide&#39;">aɪ</span><span title="/./: syllable break">.</span><span title="/æ/: &#39;a&#39; in &#39;bad&#39;">æ</span><span title="&#39;m&#39; in &#39;my&#39;">m</span><span title="/ˈ/: primary stress follows">ˈ</span><span title="/iː/: &#39;ee&#39; in &#39;fleece&#39;">iː</span></span>/</a></span></span> <a href="/wiki/Help:Pronunciation_respelling_key" title="Help:Pronunciation respelling key"><i title="English pronunciation respelling">eye-am-<span style="font-size:90%">EE</span></i></a>) (often stylized as <b>IAME</b> or <b>iAMe</b>), is an American <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop</a> recording artist, record producer and record executive from <a href="/wiki/Portland,_Oregon" title="Portland, Oregon">Portland, Oregon</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is a member of the hip hop groups <a href="/wiki/Oldominion" title="Oldominion">Oldominion</a> and Sandpeople.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In 2012, he released the album <i>Lame$tream,</i> which was given favorable reviews including <a href="/wiki/Willamette_Week" title="Willamette Week">Willamette Week</a>, who on said "This is an MC at the very top of his game" and the albums's songs "shines a light on the MC's powerful grasp of storytelling."<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He has shared stages with <a href="/wiki/Grayskul" title="Grayskul">Grayskul</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sleep_(rapper)" title="Sleep (rapper)">Sleep</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_Chicharones" title="The Chicharones">The Chicharones</a> and <a href="/wiki/Boom_Bap_Project" title="Boom Bap Project">Boom Bap Project</a>, among many others.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Musical_career">Musical career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Iame_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Musical career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>During Iame's early musical career, he released the album <i>Paradise Lost</i> in 2003 with the short lived group Redshield. This album caught the attention of notable <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music_in_the_Pacific_Northwest" title="Hip hop music in the Pacific Northwest">Pacific Northwest hip hop</a> artists <a href="/wiki/Onry_Ozzborn" title="Onry Ozzborn">Onry Ozzborn</a> and <a href="/wiki/Grayskul" title="Grayskul">JFK aka Ninjaface</a>, who would later invite Iame to join <a href="/wiki/Oldominion" title="Oldominion">Oldominion</a> in 2005.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Iame is also part of the group Sandpeople, a group consisting of ten members, one of which being <a href="/wiki/Scribble_Jam" title="Scribble Jam">Scribble Jam</a> champion <a href="/wiki/Illmaculate" title="Illmaculate">Illmaculate</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Following <i>Paradise</i>, he started an independent hip hop label named Heaven Noise.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2009, he released the album I Am My Enemy. The album reached #3 on the CMJ hip hop charts, behind only <a href="/wiki/Mos_Def" title="Mos Def">Mos Def</a> and <a href="/wiki/Eyedea_%26_Abilities" title="Eyedea &amp; Abilities">Eyedea &amp; Abilities</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In 2010, he released the album Lightfighter.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In 2011, Iame started a Kickstarted campaign to fund his new project <i>Lame</i>. The album features <a href="/wiki/Solillaquists_of_Sound" title="Solillaquists of Sound">Solillaquists Of Sound</a> and <a href="/wiki/Oldominion" title="Oldominion">Xperience</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The next year, he released the album <i>Lame$tream</i> and the mixtape <i>The Deaf Kid Mixtape</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
