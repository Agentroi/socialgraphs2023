<p><b>Arif Zahir Lopes-Thrower</b> (born April 15, 1994) is an American actor, musician, and internet personality. His YouTube channel, <b>Azerrz</b>, has over 7 million subscribers<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and features voice impressions of a plethora of celebrities and cartoon characters. Since 2021, Zahir has voiced <a href="/wiki/Cleveland_Brown" title="Cleveland Brown">Cleveland Brown</a> on the television series <i><a href="/wiki/Family_Guy" title="Family Guy">Family Guy</a></i>, succeeding <a href="/wiki/Mike_Henry_(voice_actor)" title="Mike Henry (voice actor)">Mike Henry</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Arif_Zahir&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zahir is of African-American and Cape Verdean descent and was born on April 15, 1994, in <a href="/wiki/Berkeley,_California" title="Berkeley, California">Berkeley, California</a>. He was partially raised in <a href="/wiki/New_Bedford,_Massachusetts" title="New Bedford, Massachusetts">New Bedford</a>, <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a> where he attended Nativity Preparatory School and Keith Middle School before moving back to California in 2007.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Zahir began doing voice impressions in middle school to impress his classmates. He originally made a YouTube channel in 2007 which focused on performing magic tricks.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> After doing so and finding success he started attending <a href="/wiki/Hussian_School_of_Art" class="mw-redirect" title="Hussian School of Art">Hussian College's</a> campus in Los Angeles. He later dropped out to spend more time focusing on his YouTube career.<sup id="cite_ref-variety.com_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-variety.com-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Arif_Zahir&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Zahir created his YouTube channel in 2012 but posted his first video in 2013.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He started out by posting comedic sketches, gaming videos, and videos of himself performing voice impressions of both cartoon characters and real celebrities.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Zahir garnered initial success through his voice impressions which he started doing in 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> One of the first voices Zahir did was of <a href="/wiki/Cleveland_Brown" title="Cleveland Brown">Cleveland Brown</a> who he would go on to voice several years later.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Zahir landed his first acting role playing a character named Arif in the short film <i>No One But Lydia.</i> Starting in 2018, he became a recurring cast member on <i><a href="/wiki/How_It_Should_Have_Ended" title="How It Should Have Ended">How It Should Have Ended</a></i> voicing <a href="/wiki/Black_Panther_(character)" title="Black Panther (character)">Black Panther / T'Challa</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-screenrant.com_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-screenrant.com-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Through community challenges and his first viral hit with "Ted Plays Call of Duty" (which has over 20M+ views), Arif quickly grew his channel to over 6.5+ million subscribers and 650+ million views.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>He is also a rapper and singer and releases music under the moniker <b>4rif</b>.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> He released his debut album <i>At Last Sight</i> on August 30, 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> He followed it up with an <a href="/wiki/Extended_play" title="Extended play">EP</a> called <i>Go Gadget/Blues Clues</i> which was released on April 29, 2020, in collaboration with rapper Dlngqnt.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On June 26, 2020, <a href="/wiki/Mike_Henry_(voice_actor)" title="Mike Henry (voice actor)">Mike Henry</a>, who had voiced Cleveland Brown since 1999, announced he was stepping down and believed a person of color should voice the role.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> On September 25, 2020, it was announced that Zahir would be voicing Cleveland starting in Season 20 of <i><a href="/wiki/Family_Guy" title="Family Guy">Family Guy</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
