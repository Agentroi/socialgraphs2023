<p><b>Aaron Pointer</b>, better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Abstract Rude</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> from <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles,_California" class="mw-redirect" title="Los Angeles, California">Los Angeles, California</a>.
</p><p>He is the <a href="/wiki/Master_of_Ceremonies" class="mw-redirect" title="Master of Ceremonies">MC</a> of Abstract Tribe Unique, alongside dancers Zulu Butterfly and Irie Lion King, <a href="/wiki/Turntablism" title="Turntablism">turntablist</a> DJ Drez and <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">producer</a> Fat Jack.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is also a member of <a href="/wiki/Haiku_D%27Etat" class="mw-redirect" title="Haiku D&#39;Etat">Haiku D'Etat</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Myka_9" title="Myka 9">Myka 9</a> and <a href="/wiki/Aceyalone" title="Aceyalone">Aceyalone</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and The A-Team along with Aceyalone.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Abstract_Rude&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Aaron Pointer is one of the original spawns of L.A.'s historical <a href="/wiki/Good_Life_Cafe" title="Good Life Cafe">Good Life Cafe</a>, a renowned <a href="/wiki/Open_mic" title="Open mic">open mic</a> venue. Pointer would practice his rapping every Thursday night at Good Life. He is also a member of <a href="/wiki/Project_Blowed" title="Project Blowed">Project Blowed</a>. In 2009, Abstract Rude released the album <i><a href="/wiki/Rejuvenation_(Abstract_Rude_album)" title="Rejuvenation (Abstract Rude album)">Rejuvenation</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/Rhymesayers_Entertainment" title="Rhymesayers Entertainment">Rhymesayers Entertainment</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was entirely produced by Vitamin D.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
