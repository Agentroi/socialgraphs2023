<p><b>Roshon Bernard Fegan</b> (born October 6, 1991), known  <a href="/wiki/Mononymous_person" class="mw-redirect" title="Mononymous person">mononymously</a> as <b>Roshon</b> (stylized as <b>ROSHON</b>, formerly stylized as <b>RO SHON</b>), is an American actor, rapper, and dancer. He is best known for his role as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Shake_It_Up_characters#Ty_Blue" title="List of Shake It Up characters">Ty Blue</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel" title="Disney Channel">Disney Channel</a> original series <i><a href="/wiki/Shake_It_Up_(American_TV_series)" title="Shake It Up (American TV series)">Shake It Up</a></i> and for his role as Sander Loyer in the Disney Channel movie franchise <i><a href="/wiki/Camp_Rock" title="Camp Rock">Camp Rock</a></i> with the <a href="/wiki/Jonas_Brothers" title="Jonas Brothers">Jonas Brothers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Demi_Lovato" title="Demi Lovato">Demi Lovato</a>. He writes and produces his own music as well as working with the president of Lava/Universal Republic. In 2012, Roshon appeared on <i><a href="/wiki/Dancing_with_the_Stars" title="Dancing with the Stars">Dancing with the Stars</a></i>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roshon_Fegan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Roshon Bernard Fegan was born on October 6, 1991, in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-The_Manilla_Bulletin_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-The_Manilla_Bulletin-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Amazon.com_-_Camp_Rock_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Amazon.com_-_Camp_Rock-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> His father is <a href="/wiki/African-American" class="mw-redirect" title="African-American">African-American</a> actor and producer <a href="/wiki/Roy_Fegan" title="Roy Fegan">Roy Fegan</a>, whose television credits include <i><a href="/wiki/The_Shield_(TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="The Shield (TV series)">The Shield</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Married..._with_Children" title="Married... with Children">Married... with Children</a></i>, <i>The Meteor Man</i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Will_%26_Grace" title="Will &amp; Grace">Will &amp; Grace</a></i>, among others. His mother is <a href="/wiki/Filipino_people" class="mw-redirect" title="Filipino people">Filipino</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-The_Manilla_Bulletin_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-The_Manilla_Bulletin-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ABS-CBN_News_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ABS-CBN_News-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Raised in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, Roshon Fegan had studied at the BK Acting Studio and the USC 32nd Street Performing Arts School as well as attending Tom Bradley Elementary then Hollywood High School, Before leaving to be <a href="/wiki/Home-schooled" class="mw-redirect" title="Home-schooled">home-schooled</a> to accommodate his acting career.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roshon_Fegan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Fegan began his acting career at the age of 12 with a small role in the 2004 feature film <i><a href="/wiki/Spider-Man_2" title="Spider-Man 2">Spider-Man 2</a></i>, followed by an appearance in 2006 on the TV series <i><a href="/wiki/Monk_(TV_series)" title="Monk (TV series)">Monk</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> In 2008, he appeared in the comedy film <i><a href="/wiki/Drillbit_Taylor" title="Drillbit Taylor">Drillbit Taylor</a></i> and the <a href="/wiki/VC_FilmFest" class="mw-redirect" title="VC FilmFest">VC FilmFest Award</a> winning film <i><a href="/wiki/Baby_(2008_film)" class="mw-redirect" title="Baby (2008 film)">Baby</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> In June 2008, Fegan gained popular notoriety as Sander Loyer in the <a href="/wiki/Disney_Channel_Original_Movie" class="mw-redirect" title="Disney Channel Original Movie">Disney Channel</a> original movie <i><a href="/wiki/Camp_Rock" title="Camp Rock">Camp Rock</a></i>, which was the number one entertainment telecast on cable TV in 2008,<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-The_New_York_Times_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-The_New_York_Times-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> with the Jonas Brothers. He also performed songs on the <i><a href="/wiki/Camp_Rock_(soundtrack)" title="Camp Rock (soundtrack)">Camp Rock</a></i> soundtrack  as well as the DVD bonus track <i>Camp Rock</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Amazon.com_-_Camp_Rock_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Amazon.com_-_Camp_Rock-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In 2010, Fegan reprised his role as Sander in <i><a href="/wiki/Camp_Rock_2:_The_Final_Jam" title="Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam">Camp Rock 2: The Final Jam</a></i>, the sequel to <i>Camp Rock</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-The_Manilla_Bulletin_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-The_Manilla_Bulletin-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ABS-CBN_News_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ABS-CBN_News-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Youtube.com_-_RadioDisney_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Youtube.com_-_RadioDisney-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Roshon also co-starred on the Disney Channel original series <i><a href="/wiki/Shake_It_Up_(U.S._TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="Shake It Up (U.S. TV series)">Shake It Up!</a></i> as Ty Blue, Rocky's older brother.
</p><p>Fegan was featured on the <a href="/wiki/Dancing_with_the_Stars_(U.S._season_14)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dancing with the Stars (U.S. season 14)">14th season</a> of the <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a> show <i><a href="/wiki/Dancing_with_the_Stars_(U.S._TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="Dancing with the Stars (U.S. TV series)">Dancing with the Stars</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Chelsie_Hightower" title="Chelsie Hightower">Chelsie Hightower</a> was announced as Roshon's ballroom dance partner. He stayed on for eight weeks and was placed in the top 6.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Roshon_Fegan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Roshon is a songwriter, producer, actor, artist and freestyle dancer who learned his moves by watching <a href="/wiki/Michael_Jackson" title="Michael Jackson">Michael Jackson</a> and other influential dancers. A drummer since the age of two, Roshon also plays the piano and guitar.<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
He has released a number of singles on <a href="/wiki/ITunes" title="ITunes">iTunes</a> and has finished his first self-produced EP <i>I AM RO SHON</i> under his own label, "3inaRo Entertainment".<sup id="cite_ref-Medianet:_Disney_Channel_1-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Medianet:_Disney_Channel-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-iTunes_-_3inaRo_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-iTunes_-_3inaRo-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-iTunes_-_ROSHON_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-iTunes_-_ROSHON-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The name <i>3inaRo</i> (pronounced "three-in-a-row") is a reference to being a triple-threat entertainer in his three passions: acting, freestyle dancing, and music.<sup id="cite_ref-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Philippine_Daily_Inquirer-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He currently lives in <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a> with most of his family.
He was in a relationship with dancer Dytto.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (October 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
