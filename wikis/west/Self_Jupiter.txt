<p><b>Ornette Glenn</b> (born March 16, 1970), better known by his stage name <b>Self Jupiter</b>, is an American rapper from <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He is a member of <a href="/wiki/Freestyle_Fellowship" title="Freestyle Fellowship">Freestyle Fellowship</a> alongside <a href="/wiki/Myka_9" title="Myka 9">Myka 9</a>, <a href="/wiki/Peace_(rapper)" title="Peace (rapper)">P.E.A.C.E.</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Aceyalone" title="Aceyalone">Aceyalone</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> He is also one half of The Kleenrz alongside <a href="/wiki/Kenny_Segal" title="Kenny Segal">Kenny Segal</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Self_Jupiter&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Self Jupiter released a collaborative album with producer <a href="/wiki/Kenny_Segal" title="Kenny Segal">Kenny Segal</a>, titled <i>The Kleenrz</i>, on <a href="/wiki/Hellfyre_Club" title="Hellfyre Club">Hellfyre Club</a> in 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> It featured guest appearances from <a href="/wiki/Subtitle_(rapper)" title="Subtitle (rapper)">Subtitle</a>, <a href="/wiki/Murs_(rapper)" title="Murs (rapper)">Murs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Volume_10_(rapper)" title="Volume 10 (rapper)">Volume 10</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Abstract_Rude" title="Abstract Rude">Abstract Rude</a>, among others.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The duo's second album, titled <i>Season 2</i>, was released on The Order Label in 2016.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> In 2019, Self Jupiter released a studio album, titled <i>Sexy Beast</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
