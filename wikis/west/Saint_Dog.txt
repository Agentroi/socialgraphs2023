<p><b>Steven Thronson</b> (October 21, 1975&#160;– October 13, 2020<sup id="cite_ref-auto_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>), better known by his stage name <b>Saint Dog</b>, was an American rapper from <a href="/wiki/Southern_California" title="Southern California">Southern California</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Saint_Dog&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He was an original founding member of <a href="/wiki/Kottonmouth_Kings" title="Kottonmouth Kings">Kottonmouth Kings</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> along with <a href="/wiki/D-Loc" title="D-Loc">D-Loc</a> and <a href="/wiki/Johnny_Richter" title="Johnny Richter">Johnny Richter</a>. He contributed to their first three releases <i><a href="/wiki/Stoners_Reeking_Havoc" title="Stoners Reeking Havoc">Stoners Reeking Havoc</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Royal_Highness_(album)" title="Royal Highness (album)">Royal Highness</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Hidden_Stash" title="Hidden Stash">Hidden Stash</a></i>, as well as the latest Kottonmouth album <i>Kingdom Come</i>. On August 2, 2019, Saint Dog released his third and final solo album <i>Bozo</i> via <a href="/wiki/Suburban_Noize_Records" title="Suburban Noize Records">Suburban Noize Records</a> &amp; Force 5 Records. On October 13, 2020, it was confirmed by his management that Saint Dog had died on that day, eight days short from his 45th birthday.
</p>
