<p><b>Otis Oliver Flores</b> (born June 18, 1987), better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>O.T. Genasis</b>, is an American rapper, actor and singer.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=O.T._Genasis&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Otis Oliver Flores was born on June 18, 1987, in <a href="/wiki/Atlanta" title="Atlanta">Atlanta</a> to <a href="/wiki/Garifuna" title="Garifuna">Garifuna</a> parents from <a href="/wiki/Belize" title="Belize">Belize</a>. He grew up in <a href="/wiki/Long_Beach,_California" title="Long Beach, California">Long Beach, California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Genasis states that his influences include <a href="/wiki/Cutty_Ranks" title="Cutty Ranks">Cutty Ranks</a>, <a href="/wiki/Buju_Banton" title="Buju Banton">Buju Banton</a>, <a href="/wiki/T.I." title="T.I.">T.I.</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ludacris" title="Ludacris">Ludacris</a>, <a href="/wiki/Tupac_Shakur" title="Tupac Shakur">Tupac Shakur</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shabba_Ranks" title="Shabba Ranks">Shabba Ranks</a>, and <a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=O.T._Genasis&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>In 2011, <a href="/wiki/G-Unit_Records" title="G-Unit Records">G-Unit Records</a> signed him to their label. In 2012, under his association with G-Unit, he released his untitled debut official mixtape. While doing a number of small local shows in Los Angeles Ca. With his dj, DJ Necterr at the time. He caught the attention of Busta Rhymes. He later was signed to <a href="/wiki/Busta_Rhymes" title="Busta Rhymes">Busta Rhymes</a>' <a href="/wiki/Conglomerate_(record_label)" title="Conglomerate (record label)">Conglomerate Records</a>. After being signed to Conglomerate Records label, he has been putting out some songs and singles such as "Touchdown (Remix)" featuring Busta Rhymes and <a href="/wiki/French_Montana" title="French Montana">French Montana</a> and "<a href="/wiki/CoCo_(O.T._Genasis_song)" title="CoCo (O.T. Genasis song)">CoCo</a>". The latter had success on the charts, peaking at number 20 on the US <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>. <a href="/wiki/Beyonc%C3%A9" title="Beyoncé">Beyoncé</a> used his song "Everybody Mad" as part of her <a href="/wiki/Coachella_Valley_Music_and_Arts_Festival" class="mw-redirect" title="Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival">Coachella</a> 2018 performance and as part of her and <a href="/wiki/Jay-Z" title="Jay-Z">Jay-Z</a>'s <a href="/wiki/On_the_Run_II_Tour" title="On the Run II Tour">On the Run II Tour</a>.
</p><p>In 2022, "I Look Good" was featured prominently in the "Doin' Laps" commercial for the <a href="/wiki/IPhone_13" title="IPhone 13">iPhone 13</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=O.T._Genasis&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Flores is a father to son Genasis Flores, who was born on March 25, 2010. In a 2016 interview with <a href="/wiki/The_Shade_Room" title="The Shade Room">The Shade Room</a>, O.T. stated his son has autism. On March 14, 2020,  Malika Haqq, an actress and TV personality, delivered their baby boy Ace Flores.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
