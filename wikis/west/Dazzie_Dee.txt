<p><b>Dazzie Dee</b> is an American <a href="/wiki/West_Coast_hip_hop" title="West Coast hip hop">West Coast hip hop</a> <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> and record producer who has collaborated and affiliated with artists such as <a href="/wiki/Suga_Free" title="Suga Free">Suga Free</a>, Mausberg, <a href="/wiki/DJ_Quik" title="DJ Quik">DJ Quik</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ice_Cube" title="Ice Cube">Ice Cube</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shade_Sheist" title="Shade Sheist">Shade Sheist</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/K-Dee" title="K-Dee">K-Dee</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kool_G_Rap" title="Kool G Rap">Kool G Rap</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coolio" title="Coolio">Coolio</a>, Mixmaster Spade, <a href="/wiki/Yo-Yo_(rapper)" title="Yo-Yo (rapper)">Yo-Yo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Da_Lench_Mob" title="Da Lench Mob">Da Lench Mob</a>, and many more. He released two solo albums in 1996, <i>Where's My Receipt?</i> and <i>The Re-Birth</i>, with the most known singles "Knee Deep", "Everybody Wants To Be A Gangsta", and "Where You From?".<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dazzie_Dee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dazzie_Dee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Lidell Williams was born on November 30, Los Angeles, California. He attended and graduated from <a href="/wiki/Washington_Preparatory_High_School" title="Washington Preparatory High School">Washington Preparatory High School</a> in 1989. At 14 Williams met <a href="/wiki/Dr._Dre" title="Dr. Dre">Dr. Dre</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sir_Jinx" title="Sir Jinx">Sir Jinx</a> and K-Dee. He's been a <a href="/wiki/Gang" title="Gang">gang</a> member of 107th Street UG/BC <a href="/wiki/Crips" title="Crips">Crips</a> set,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> according to Sheppard, member of <a href="/wiki/Skool_Boyz" title="Skool Boyz">Skool Boyz</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> His first EP <i>Turn It Loose</i> was produced by Dr. Dre and Sir Jinx. He was one of the original members of the Lench Mob records, which it was formed by Ice Cube. Due to artistic differences, he left the label to record his first solo album <i>Where's My Receipt</i> released on <a href="/wiki/Capitol_Records" title="Capitol Records">Capitol Records</a> in 1995. Then he made a <a href="/wiki/Re-issue" class="mw-redirect" title="Re-issue">re-issue</a> for the album as <i>The Re-Birth</i> on Raging Bull Records in 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> It was produced by DJ Battlecat and had guest appearances from <a href="/wiki/Ice_Cube" title="Ice Cube">Ice Cube</a>, <a href="/wiki/Coolio" title="Coolio">Coolio</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> K-Dee and Tha Chill from <a href="/wiki/Compton%27s_Most_Wanted" title="Compton&#39;s Most Wanted">Compton's Most Wanted</a>. It's considered by many as a West Coast classic.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="With_the_Lench_Mob">With the Lench Mob</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dazzie_Dee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: With the Lench Mob">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dazzie Dee (formerly known as Big <i>"Saccs"</i>),<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/G-funk" title="G-funk">G-funk</a> rapper and an original former member of the legendary West Coast record label <a href="/wiki/Lench_Mob_Records" title="Lench Mob Records">Lench Mob</a> along with Ice Cube, K-Dee,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> J-Dee, Yo-Yo, <a href="/wiki/Chilly_Chill" title="Chilly Chill">Chilly Chill</a>, T-Bone, Shorty and more.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Due to artistic differences, he left the label to record his first solo album <i>Where's My Receipt</i> released on <a href="/wiki/Capitol_Records" title="Capitol Records">Capitol Records</a> in 1995.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Then he made a <a href="/wiki/Re-issue" class="mw-redirect" title="Re-issue">re-issue</a> for the album as <i>The Re-Birth</i> on Raging Bull Records in 1996.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Dazzie_Dee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Killafornia Organization was a Hip hop supergroup that consisted of <a href="/wiki/South_Central_Cartel" title="South Central Cartel">South Central Cartel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Compton%27s_Most_Wanted" title="Compton&#39;s Most Wanted">Compton's Most Wanted</a>, Bonaphyde, Dazzie Dee, Killa, <a href="/wiki/Tray_Deee" class="mw-redirect" title="Tray Deee">Tray Deee</a>, Hard Times &amp; Wize Guyz. Their debut album, <i>Killafornia Organization</i>, was released in 1996 by Killa Cali Records, Thug Records and produced by 3 Strikes Prod.
</p>
