<p><b>Na-Kel Allah Smith</b>  (born July 22, 1994) is an American skateboarder, rapper, songwriter and actor.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Originally a skateboarder from the <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a> area, he was known for his association with the <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_collective" class="mw-redirect" title="Hip hop collective">hip hop collective</a> <a href="/wiki/Odd_Future" title="Odd Future">Odd Future</a>, appearing in music videos, vlogs and their <a href="/wiki/Adult_Swim" title="Adult Swim">Adult Swim</a> TV show <i><a href="/wiki/Loiter_Squad" title="Loiter Squad">Loiter Squad</a></i>, and performing live with the collective and its members.<sup id="cite_ref-pfork_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pfork-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Na-Kel_Smith&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Smith debuted his music career in 2013, marking his first recorded verse on <a href="/wiki/Tyler,_the_Creator" title="Tyler, the Creator">Tyler, the Creator</a>'s second studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Wolf_(Tyler,_the_Creator_album)" title="Wolf (Tyler, the Creator album)">Wolf</a></i> on the <a href="/wiki/Posse_cut" title="Posse cut">posse cut</a> "Trashwang". In 2014, after his appearance in the <a href="/wiki/Supreme_(clothing)" class="mw-redirect" title="Supreme (clothing)">Supreme</a> video "<i>Cherry</i>", Smith co-founded his own hardware/apparel company, Hardies, with teammate <a href="/wiki/Tyshawn_Jones" title="Tyshawn Jones">Tyshawn Jones</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-mc_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-mc-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In 2015, he formed Hog Slaughta Boyz, a duo composed of Smith and former Odd Future member <a href="/wiki/Earl_Sweatshirt" title="Earl Sweatshirt">Earl Sweatshirt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fader_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fader-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In 2018, Smith starred in his first commercial film, <i><a href="/wiki/Mid90s" title="Mid90s">Mid90s</a></i>, directed by <a href="/wiki/Jonah_Hill" title="Jonah Hill">Jonah Hill</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-nyt_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nyt-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In 2019, Smith released his debut studio album <i>Twothousand Nakteen</i> through A Dream No Longer Deferred Records and Narcowave.<sup id="cite_ref-pfork_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pfork-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Smith is the nephew of professional skateboarder <a href="/wiki/Kareem_Campbell" title="Kareem Campbell">Kareem Campbell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
