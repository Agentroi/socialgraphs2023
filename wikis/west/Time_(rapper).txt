					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" lang="en" dir="ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">American rapper</div>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1126788409">.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul{line-height:inherit;list-style:none;margin:0;padding:0}.mw-parser-output .plainlist ol li,.mw-parser-output .plainlist ul li{margin-bottom:0}</style><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r1066479718">.mw-parser-output .infobox-subbox{padding:0;border:none;margin:-3px;width:auto;min-width:100%;font-size:100%;clear:none;float:none;background-color:transparent}.mw-parser-output .infobox-3cols-child{margin:auto}.mw-parser-output .infobox .navbar{font-size:100%}body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-header,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-subheader,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-above,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-title,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-image,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-full-data,body.skin-minerva .mw-parser-output .infobox-below{text-align:center}</style><table class="infobox vcard plainlist"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-above" style="background-color: #b0c4de; font-size: 125%;"><div class="fn">Time</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Birth name</span></th><td class="infobox-data nickname">Christopher Steele</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Born</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Denver" title="Denver">Denver</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colorado" title="Colorado">Colorado</a>, U.S.</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Genres</th><td class="infobox-data"><a href="/wiki/Alternative_hip_hop" title="Alternative hip hop">Alternative hip hop</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Occupation(s)</th><td class="infobox-data role"><a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">Rapper</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Instrument(s)</th><td class="infobox-data note"><a href="/wiki/Musical_keyboard" title="Musical keyboard">Keyboard</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label"><span class="nowrap">Years active</span></th><td class="infobox-data">2000-present</td></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Labels</th><td class="infobox-data">Dirty Laboratory Productions</td></tr><tr><th colspan="2" class="infobox-header" style="background-color: #b0c4de"></th></tr><tr><th scope="row" class="infobox-label">Website</th><td class="infobox-data"><span class="url"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://thisistime.bandcamp.com">thisistime<wbr />.bandcamp<wbr />.com</a></span></td></tr></tbody></table><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">Musical artist</div>
<p><b>Christopher Steele</b>, better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Time</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Indie_hip_hop" class="mw-redirect" title="Indie hip hop">indie hip hop</a> artist from <a href="/wiki/Denver" title="Denver">Denver</a>, <a href="/wiki/Colorado" title="Colorado">Colorado</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-westword2013_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-westword2013-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is one of the original members of Dirty Laboratory Productions.<sup id="cite_ref-westword2013_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-westword2013-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is also one half of the hip hop group <a href="/wiki/Calm_(music_group)" title="Calm (music group)">Calm</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-westword2013_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-westword2013-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Aside from music, Steele is also an investigative journalist.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Time_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Time released his first album, <i>Litterture</i>, in 2004.<sup id="cite_ref-westword2013_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-westword2013-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He then went on to release the album, <i>Anti-Smiles</i>, in 2006 under the moniker <a href="/wiki/Calm_(music_group)" title="Calm (music group)">Calm</a> with producer Awareness.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/Denver_Westword" class="mw-redirect" title="Denver Westword">Denver Westword</a></i> gave the album a favorable review, commenting that "[although] <i>Anti-Smiles</i> reflects the dark side of our collective psyche, it's also a statement of hope in the face of fear and self-doubt."<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In 2007, he released his solo album, <i>The Fantastic Reality</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <i>Benzine Magazine</i> gave the album 3 out of 5.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Time currently serves as an associate professor of History at <a href="/wiki/Regis_University" title="Regis University">Regis University</a> in Denver, Colorado.  Time's scholarly areas include politics and tort law, ethics and social justice, pedagogy, and the history of social change.  Steele received a Doctor's of Jurisprudence (J.D.,) from Creighton University.  Time teaches courses on History and Social Change.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Time released his solo album, <i><a href="/wiki/Naked_Dinner" title="Naked Dinner">Naked Dinner</a></i>, in 2009.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> It featured guest appearances from Damon Jevon, <a href="/wiki/Sole_(hip_hop_artist)" title="Sole (hip hop artist)">Sole</a>, <a href="/wiki/C-Rayz_Walz" title="C-Rayz Walz">C-Rayz Walz</a> and Extra Kool.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The album was described by <i>Denver Westword</i> as "a dark affair tonally reminiscent of <i><a href="/wiki/There%27s_a_Riot_Goin%27_On" title="There&#39;s a Riot Goin&#39; On">There's a Riot Goin' On</a></i>, with a similar socially critical agenda."<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2010, Time released 12 EPs in 12 months called the <i>12 Headed Hydra</i> series for free download.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> In 2013, he released <i><a href="/wiki/Newstalgia" title="Newstalgia">Newstalgia</a></i>, an album featuring contributions from the likes of <a href="/wiki/Ceschi" title="Ceschi">Ceschi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jake_One" title="Jake One">Jake One</a>, <a href="/wiki/Factor_(producer)" class="mw-redirect" title="Factor (producer)">Factor</a>, Budo, Man Mantis, and <a href="/wiki/Xiu_Xiu" title="Xiu Xiu">Xiu Xiu</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a journalist, Chris Steele has written for <i><a href="/wiki/Examiner.com" title="Examiner.com">The Examiner</a></i> as a progressive politics reporter.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> He has written on topics ranging from UFOs to the death of <a href="/wiki/Fred_Hampton" title="Fred Hampton">Fred Hampton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> In 2011, he has received attention for writing in favor of the <a href="/wiki/Occupy_Wall_Street" title="Occupy Wall Street">Occupy Wall Street</a> movement and was interviewed on Mike Feder's XM radio show <i>Occupied Territory</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Style_and_influences">Style and influences</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Time_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Style and influences">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
