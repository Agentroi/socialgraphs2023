<p><b>Ritchie Fontaine</b>, also known as <b>Lil Ric</b> is an American rapper from <a href="/wiki/Richmond,_California" title="Richmond, California">Richmond, California</a>, United States, formerly of <a href="/wiki/No_Limit_Records" title="No Limit Records">No Limit</a> and <a href="/wiki/Noo_Trybe_Records" title="Noo Trybe Records">Noo Trybe Records</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Lil Ric has worked with numerous other Californian rappers including <a href="/wiki/Laroo_T.H.H." class="mw-redirect" title="Laroo T.H.H.">Laroo</a>, <a href="/wiki/JT_the_Bigga_Figga" title="JT the Bigga Figga">JT the Bigga Figga</a>, <a href="/wiki/Keak_da_Sneak" title="Keak da Sneak">Keak da Sneak</a>,  <a href="/wiki/San_Quinn" title="San Quinn">San Quinn</a> and <a href="/wiki/The_Jacka" title="The Jacka">The Jacka</a>.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_career">Music career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Lil_Ric&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>On October 11, 1994, Lil Ric would release his debut album titled <i><a href="/wiki/Deep_n_tha_Game" class="mw-redirect" title="Deep n tha Game">Deep n tha Game</a></i>, it would be one of <a href="/wiki/No_Limit_Records" title="No Limit Records">No Limit</a>'s first album releases. After disbanding from No Limit on May 14, 1996, Lil Ric would release his second album titled <i><a href="/wiki/Wicked_Streets" class="mw-redirect" title="Wicked Streets">Wicked Streets</a></i> via Solo Records. On August 8, 2000, Lil Ric would release his third album titled <i>It's Like Armageddon</i> via A.W.O.L., <a href="/wiki/Noo_Trybe_Records" title="Noo Trybe Records">Noo Trybe</a>, <a href="/wiki/Virgin_Records" title="Virgin Records">Virgin</a>. On February 26, 2001, Fiend would release his fourth album titled <i>The Thug Nut (On One)</i> via Concrete Music. On August 28, 2012, Lil Ric would release his fifth album titled <i>Plugged In - Live from the Streets</i> via Ae'on Music Group Rapbay, Urbanlife Distribution, it would be his first album release in 11 years.
</p>
