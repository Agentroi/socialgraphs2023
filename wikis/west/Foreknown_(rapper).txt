<p><b>Gary C. Bagby</b> (born August 20, 1979), who goes by the stage name <b>Foreknown</b> and formerly <b>Foreknown Apostolic</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Christian_hip_hop" title="Christian hip hop">Christian hip hop</a> rapper. He started making <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop music</a> in 2009, with his first <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" class="mw-redirect" title="Studio album">studio album</a>, <i>Ornithology</i>, releasing in 2014 from Humble Beast Records.
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life">Early life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Foreknown_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gary C. Bagby was born on August 20, 1979,<sup id="cite_ref-BDAY_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BDAY-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and raised in <a href="/wiki/Alhambra,_California" title="Alhambra, California">Alhambra, California</a> by his parents, where he graduated from <a href="/wiki/Alhambra_High_School_(Alhambra,_California)" title="Alhambra High School (Alhambra, California)">Alhambra High School</a> in 1998.<sup id="cite_ref-LA_Times_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LA_Times-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> He is an avid <i><a href="/wiki/Star_Wars" title="Star Wars">Star Wars</a></i> fanatic.<sup id="cite_ref-Hope_AUS_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hope_AUS-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_career">Music career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Foreknown_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Music career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>His <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_music" title="Hip hop music">hip hop music</a> recording career began in 2009,<sup id="cite_ref-About_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-About-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> while his first <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" class="mw-redirect" title="Studio album">studio album</a>, <i>Ornithology</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-CR_Review_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CR_Review-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> was released on August 12, 2014, by Humble Beast Records.<sup id="cite_ref-JFH_Review_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JFH_Review-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-RG_Review_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RG_Review-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Foreknown_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>He resides in <a href="/wiki/Peoria,_Arizona" title="Peoria, Arizona">Peoria, Arizona</a>, with his wife and children.<sup id="cite_ref-About_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-About-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
