<p><b>Terrence Louis Henderson Jr.</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>Punch</b>, is an American rapper, songwriter, record producer and record executive from <a href="/wiki/Carson,_California" title="Carson, California">Carson, California</a>. He is best known for being the <a href="/wiki/President_(corporate_title)" title="President (corporate title)">president</a> of independent record label <a href="/wiki/Top_Dawg_Entertainment" title="Top Dawg Entertainment">Top Dawg Entertainment</a> (TDE).<sup id="cite_ref-Tardio_2016_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Tardio_2016-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Ivey_2017_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Ivey_2017-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> He has also executive produced several successful albums, including <a href="/wiki/Kendrick_Lamar" title="Kendrick Lamar">Kendrick Lamar</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Section.80" title="Section.80">Section.80</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> (2011), <a href="/wiki/Schoolboy_Q" title="Schoolboy Q">Schoolboy Q</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Habits_%26_Contradictions" title="Habits &amp; Contradictions">Habits &amp; Contradictions</a></i><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> (2012), <a href="/wiki/Terrace_Martin" title="Terrace Martin">Terrace Martin</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/3ChordFold" title="3ChordFold">3ChordFold</a></i>,<sup id="cite_ref-3ch_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3ch-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> (2013) and more. Aside from his solo musical career, Punch is a member of <a href="/wiki/Hip_hop_collective" class="mw-redirect" title="Hip hop collective">hip hop collective</a> A Room Full of Mirrors, alongside fellow rappers Daylyt, Nick Grant, <a href="/wiki/Willie_B_(producer)" title="Willie B (producer)">The Ichiban Don</a>, Lyric Michelle, Hari, and more.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Punch_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Terrence Henderson founded the Carson, California-based independent record label <a href="/wiki/Top_Dawg_Entertainment" title="Top Dawg Entertainment">Top Dawg Entertainment</a> (TDE) in 2004, with Anthony "Top Dawg" Tiffith and <a href="/wiki/Dave_Free" title="Dave Free">Dave "Miyatola" Free</a>. He then began his musical career in 2007 under the <a href="/wiki/Moniker" class="mw-redirect" title="Moniker">moniker</a> Punch. In March 2016, Punch revealed plans to release his debut <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" class="mw-redirect" title="Studio album">studio album</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DA_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DA-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Punch told <i><a href="/wiki/Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i> that he plans on doing "a project for sure", even though his artistic process has usually come in spurts: "I think I’m going to finally finish it because I write a lot and I record every so often. I think I’m just gonna go ahead and knock it out at this point."<sup id="cite_ref-DA_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DA-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> On December 5, 2016, Punch released a single titled "Gold".<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
