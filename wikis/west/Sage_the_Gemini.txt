<p><b>Dominic Wynn Woods</b><sup id="cite_ref-complex-cosign_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-complex-cosign-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> (born June 20, 1992),<sup id="cite_ref-allmusic_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-allmusic-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> better known by his stage name <b>Sage the Gemini</b> (<span class="rt-commentedText nowrap"><span class="IPA nopopups noexcerpt" lang="en-fonipa"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/English" title="Help:IPA/English">/<span style="border-bottom:1px dotted"><span title="&#39;s&#39; in &#39;sigh&#39;">s</span><span title="/eɪ/: &#39;a&#39; in &#39;face&#39;">eɪ</span><span title="/dʒ/: &#39;j&#39; in &#39;jam&#39;">dʒ</span></span>/</a></span></span>), is an American rapper and record producer from <a href="/wiki/Fairfield,_California" title="Fairfield, California">Fairfield, California</a>. He is perhaps best known for his 2013 singles "<a href="/wiki/Gas_Pedal" title="Gas Pedal">Gas Pedal</a>" (featuring <a href="/wiki/Iamsu!" title="Iamsu!">Iamsu!</a>), which peaked at number 29 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>, and "<a href="/wiki/Red_Nose" title="Red Nose">Red Nose</a>", as well his feature on <a href="/wiki/Flo_Rida" title="Flo Rida">Flo Rida</a>'s 2014 single "<a href="/wiki/G.D.F.R._(song)" class="mw-redirect" title="G.D.F.R. (song)">Going Down For Real (GDFR)</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-Sage_The_Gemini_–_Chart_history_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sage_The_Gemini_–_Chart_history-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> His debut studio album <i><a href="/wiki/Remember_Me_(Sage_the_Gemini_album)" title="Remember Me (Sage the Gemini album)">Remember Me</a></i> was released through <a href="/wiki/Republic_Records" title="Republic Records">Republic Records</a> in March 2014. He is a member of Bay Area hip hop collective <a href="/wiki/The_HBK_Gang" title="The HBK Gang">The HBK Gang</a>, and the only member of the group alongside singer <a href="/wiki/Kehlani" title="Kehlani">Kehlani</a> to be signed to a major label.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Life_and_career">Life and career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sage_the_Gemini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Life and career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="1992.E2.80.932012:_Early_life_and_career_beginnings"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="1992–2012:_Early_life_and_career_beginnings">1992–2012: Early life and career beginnings</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sage_the_Gemini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: 1992–2012: Early life and career beginnings">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Sage the Gemini was born on June 20, 1992, in <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco,_California" class="mw-redirect" title="San Francisco, California">San Francisco, California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Noisey_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Noisey-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> His family moved to <a href="/wiki/Fairfield,_California" title="Fairfield, California">Fairfield, California</a>, when he was 6–7 years old.<sup id="cite_ref-Noisey_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Noisey-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He started recording at the age of eleven with his brother after he bought a microphone from Gordon's Music and Sound shop in Fairfield, California. The first track he recorded, at 14, was titled "Made In China".<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Woods took the name Sage the Gemini due to the color of his eyes and his <a href="/wiki/Zodiac" title="Zodiac">zodiac</a> sign.<sup id="cite_ref-republicrecords1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-republicrecords1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hiphopdx1_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hiphopdx1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In 2008, Sage released his first single, "You Should Know" on MySpace. It became a viral hit, generating over three million views worldwide. He then joined Black Money Music Group. Being close friends with rapper <a href="/wiki/Iamsu!" title="Iamsu!">Iamsu!</a>, he also joined <a href="/wiki/The_HBK_Gang" title="The HBK Gang">The HBK Gang</a>, or the Heartbreak Gang.<sup id="cite_ref-republicrecords1_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-republicrecords1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-hiphopdx1_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hiphopdx1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span id="2013.E2.80.93present:_Remember_Me"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="2013–present:_Remember_Me">2013–present: <i>Remember Me</i></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sage_the_Gemini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: 2013–present: Remember Me">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In March 2013, Sage released the singles "<a href="/wiki/Red_Nose" title="Red Nose">Red Nose</a>" and "<a href="/wiki/Gas_Pedal" title="Gas Pedal">Gas Pedal</a>" featuring <a href="/wiki/Iamsu!" title="Iamsu!">Iamsu!</a>, the latter has reached number 29 on the US <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100"><i>Billboard</i> Hot 100</a>, making it his first top 40 hit; it also reached number six on the <a href="/wiki/Hot_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Songs" title="Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Songs">Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Songs</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Sage_The_Gemini_–_Chart_history_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Sage_The_Gemini_–_Chart_history-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The songs immediately became hits and gained substantial airplay, which persuaded <a href="/wiki/Republic_Records" title="Republic Records">Republic Records</a> to sign a deal with him in August 2013.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_America" title="Recording Industry Association of America">Recording Industry Association of America</a> certified "Red Nose" Gold and "Gas Pedal" Platinum.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> On March 25, 2014, Sage released his debut studio album, <i><a href="/wiki/Remember_Me_(Sage_the_Gemini_album)" title="Remember Me (Sage the Gemini album)">Remember Me</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-xxlmag1_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-xxlmag1-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In October 2014, he was featured on <a href="/wiki/Flo_Rida" title="Flo Rida">Flo Rida</a>'s single "<a href="/wiki/G.D.F.R._(song)" class="mw-redirect" title="G.D.F.R. (song)">G.D.F.R.</a>", which peaked at number eight on the <i>Billboard</i> Hot 100.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In July 2017, Sage released his debut mixtape, <i>Morse Code</i>, which includes the Gold certified single "<a href="/wiki/Now_and_Later_(song)" title="Now and Later (song)">Now and Later</a>".<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> "Now and Later" peaked at the #1 spot on the urban charts in Australia and the UK.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Sage_the_Gemini&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Sage was 19 when he had his first child, a daughter, in 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> He dated <a href="/wiki/Jordin_Sparks" title="Jordin Sparks">Jordin Sparks</a> for a short time in 2015.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> He has a daughter born in 2017 to an unnamed woman.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>
