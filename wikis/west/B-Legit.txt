<p><b>Brandt Jones</b> (born January 13, 1968), better known by his <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a> <b>B-Legit</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapper" class="mw-redirect" title="Rapper">rapper</a> from <a href="/wiki/Vallejo,_California" title="Vallejo, California">Vallejo</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SFW_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SFW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=B-Legit&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Before he started his music career, he went to <a href="/wiki/Grambling_State_University" title="Grambling State University">Grambling State University</a>. He became a member of <a href="/wiki/The_Click" title="The Click">The Click</a>, a rap group formed by his cousin <a href="/wiki/E-40" title="E-40">E-40</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SFW_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SFW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was featured on the track "Aint Hard 2 Find" on <a href="/wiki/2Pac" class="mw-redirect" title="2Pac">2Pac</a>'s album <i><a href="/wiki/All_Eyez_on_Me" title="All Eyez on Me">All Eyez on Me</a></i>.  He has been on several independent and major labels such as <a href="/wiki/Sick_Wid_It_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Sick Wid It Records">Sick Wid It Records</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-SFW_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SFW-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jive_Records" title="Jive Records">Jive Records</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Koch_Records" class="mw-redirect" title="Koch Records">Koch Records</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>  He has collaborated with numerous other artists including <a href="/wiki/E-40" title="E-40">E-40</a>, <a href="/wiki/Celly_Cel" title="Celly Cel">Celly Cel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Master_P" title="Master P">Master P</a>, <a href="/wiki/Too_Short" title="Too Short">Too Short</a>, <a
