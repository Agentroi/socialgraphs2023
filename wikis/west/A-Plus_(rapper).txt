<p><b>Adam Carter</b>, known by the <a href="/wiki/Stage_name" title="Stage name">stage name</a>, <b>A-Plus</b>, is an American <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> and <a href="/wiki/Record_producer" title="Record producer">producer</a>.  He is one of the founding four members of the <a href="/wiki/Oakland,_California" title="Oakland, California">Oakland, California</a>-based <a href="/wiki/Alternative_hip_hop" title="Alternative hip hop">underground hip hop</a> group <a href="/wiki/Souls_of_Mischief" title="Souls of Mischief">Souls of Mischief</a> and, with Souls of Mischief, a part of the eight-person, alternative hip hop <a href="/wiki/Musical_collective" title="Musical collective">collective</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Hieroglyphics_(band)" class="mw-redirect" title="Hieroglyphics (band)">Hieroglyphics</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Biography">Biography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=A-Plus_(rapper)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Biography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Born in <a href="/wiki/Denver,_Colorado" class="mw-redirect" title="Denver, Colorado">Denver, Colorado</a> as the son of <a href="/wiki/Jamaica" title="Jamaica">Jamaican</a> <a href="/wiki/Immigration" title="Immigration">immigrants</a>, A-Plus moved to <a href="/wiki/Oakland,_California" title="Oakland, California">Oakland, California</a> with his family when he was 5 years old.<sup id="cite_ref-Public_Service_Announcement:_A-Plus_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Public_Service_Announcement:_A-Plus-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  In <a href="/wiki/Kindergarten" title="Kindergarten">kindergarten</a>, A-Plus met future <a href="/wiki/Hieroglyphics_(band)" class="mw-redirect" title="Hieroglyphics (band)">Hieroglyphics</a> member, <a href="/wiki/Casual_(rapper)" title="Casual (rapper)">Casual</a>, and in <a href="/wiki/Elementary_school" class="mw-redirect" title="Elementary school">elementary school</a>, met fellow <a href="/wiki/Souls_of_Mischief" title="Souls of Mischief">Souls of Mischief</a> member <a href="/wiki/Tajai" title="Tajai">Tajai</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Interview:_Casual_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Interview:_Casual-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  Tajai eventually introduced A-Plus to future Souls of Mischief members <a href="/wiki/Phesto" title="Phesto">Phesto</a> and <a href="/wiki/Opio" class="mw-redirect" title="Opio">Opio</a>, and the group formed in <a href="/wiki/High_school" class="mw-redirect" title="High school">high school</a> before releasing their debut album, <i><a href="/wiki/93_%27til_Infinity" title="93 &#39;til Infinity">93 'til Infinity</a></i> on <a href="/wiki/Jive_Records" title="Jive Records">Jive Records</a> in 1993.<sup id="cite_ref-Phesto_Interview_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Phesto_Interview-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After A-Plus and the Souls of Mischief were dropped by Jive, the group was incorporated into the Hieroglyphics in 1995, at the urging of <a href="/wiki/Del_tha_Funkee_Homosapien" class="mw-redirect" title="Del tha Funkee Homosapien">Del tha Funkee Homosapien</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Phesto_Interview_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Phesto_Interview-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A-Plus has contributed to all four Souls of Mischief albums, as well as the two Hieroglyphic studio albums.  He has also produced or performed on solo projects of various Hieroglyphics members.
</p><p>In 2005, A-Plus released a <a href="/wiki/Remix" title="Remix">remix</a> album, <i>Pleemix, Vol. 1</i>, through <a href="/wiki/Hieroglyphics_Imperium_Recordings" title="Hieroglyphics Imperium Recordings">Hieroglyphics Imperium Recordings</a>. On May 1, 2007, A-Plus released his debut <a href="/wiki/Solo_album" class="mw-redirect" title="Solo album">solo album</a>, <i>My Last Good Deed</i> through <a href="/wiki/Hieroglyphics_Imperium_Recordings" title="Hieroglyphics Imperium Recordings">Hieroglyphics Imperium Recordings</a>.
</p>
